 57, (Janvier 2002) Tamazight D turjit wapa Yura t: Brapim Oadili (Zrarda, Tapla, Taza) Heddu yemmuyraw (yemnagraw) aked ict n tefransawict x webrid n lantirnit. Yemnisin akid s. Tuss d akid s ghar tamazgha. Toedl as lekwaghedv. Yin (ggin) islan (urar) d imeqranen. Tensa tarumect tetturar ahidus aked Heddu d lepl nnes d imeddukal nnes al ayen di (jusqu'à..que) tenqar d tfuct. Ttugha ten qao (marra) zepan aked ixfawen nsen ghas Heddu ttugha dt yezmummuy mghar i wul nnes. Netta lebda ytexs ad yemlec yellis n tamurt nnes, maca war yezmir. Yuhel irdzu (yarezzu) x lxedmet, walu. Z deffer (s deffar) idj n unebdu ighuda, tezzry idt (passer, ssekk) akid s tarumect. Yewwetv (yewwedv) ayud (akud) n di ghra zvwan ghar fransa. Tesmun as yemma s ihrawen nnes, teya s (tegga s) akid sen cway n tament (tamment) d wadi d zzect tabeldict huma ad issen yeswingem tamurt nnes dipa di tmura n midden. Temsafadv tefransawict aked ayt axxam nnes, maca Heddu yugg ad kid sen yemsafadv. War yezmir ad yenniy (voir) imettvawen n yemma s. Bettvu nsen yuooer xaf s. Agharrabu yteggêj x tamazgha. Heddu yuts (yebda) yetxarras di tamurt nnes d mayn (min) dag s yezzrey aked imeddukal nnes. Aytmas, yemma s, baba s wedd (wa nita) war yettvaf wix ghra yessghuy negh waked ghra yemmnegh. Tura (ruxa) yeffgh as axxam. Ttugha trumct tettsapa dt ad yettu, nzi dt ssekren ighuyyan n baba s zeg itves: “kkar ssya (ssanita) a tarwa n leghben, ku yidj mani yxeddem, cekk tejnit (tettvsed) al uyud n umcli!”. Tazwart a yennum akid s Heddu zeg ûss n di yewwey Lisens yuss d yeqqim deg wxxam, yekkar, yesserd axenfuf nnes yeffegh ghar barra yesgha anghmis nnes, yekka x umeddukel nnes rahen ad qqimen di lqepwa. Yessiwdv as x mayn (min) yurja itvlli d itran tghennajen: aghirin i waman, war dinni bu laman, war din mghar tamara d imettvawen yehman. 2002 Tawiza. .
