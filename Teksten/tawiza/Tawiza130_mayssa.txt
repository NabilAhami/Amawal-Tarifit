 uïïun 130, sinyur 2958 (Février 2008) Tamazight Jar tmewwa Tura t: Mayssa Racida Tyuri n tfuct Toedjem ayi s raxart inu Rami yemmut baba Inna yi: oad ddar, ddar Tameghrust n tudart inu Ghar tigwdi di tittvawin Negh ixf nnem di marra min yedjan inu Ruxa raxart tzadja xaf i Tebdva yi d tiseqqar, man ten idjan inu? Tarzeg xaf i tsanfunit n tfuct Umi d teyra nebra yareqqas inu Tejja yi jar tmewwa d ticemrarin War djigh n da ura n dipa inu Arekwsegh day i imettvan War yezrin temrer n tudart inu. 2002 Tawiza. .
