 Uttvun 77, Tzayûr 2003 ( 2003) Tamazight Imerwalen Yura t: Masher n A t Jadu (Libya( Kkert ad nerwel, D ad yawedv galuz, Kkert ad nazzel Akwu deffer anegh azduz nnes muhal ad nedwel newdu nnes yezghel dey tegrest ineghghel asemmidv d adfel. ***** Tella denneg adrar yun nnes qeshen am dghagh, Ghef s iwdan tvfen dvar waqila tugur tetwagh. ***** Tghessem sey s tarewla, tarewla nnwen s iman nnwen, niyyet tella sey dula lwend af lwend dey s dren. ***** Tella denneg adrar yun nnes ibab n tilelli wedden af lheq awooar terrugza nnsen tami. ***** Zedghen dey s sey zman, tegrest d newdu Agar ighusra d irjan tmeddert tetbeddel tiru rewlen sey s iwdan wel tessal d sey sen kan. Kkert ad nerwel ass u, ameckan d ameqran ad teqqim in leodu ammek yetvra zman.
