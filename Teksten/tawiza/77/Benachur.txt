 Uttvun 77, Tzayûr 2003 ( 2003) Tamazight Illel Yura t: Zapir Benoacur (Dzayer, Bgayt) Illel yeccur d aman Ul yeccur d urfan Yugi ad yestaofu. Amenzu oemrent waman Wis sin d idim i tyerban Yettwaqqed am usafu. D awal i t yerhan Qqazen t wussan Yugi ad yettu. D lsa-is rzan D ccama is ooan Wis ma ad yehlu. Tiyita is-fkan Am tuqqsa izerman I lebda ad tegru. I wid yecfan Yekfa laman D buberkan uqeru. Ur ttamen iles azidan Ur ttamen wid yettavsan A oin nsen d asuru. Tidett d is han Tirrugza d ayen yelpan D agerruj ur nrekku. (Ass 07/03/2003) 2002 Tawiza. .
