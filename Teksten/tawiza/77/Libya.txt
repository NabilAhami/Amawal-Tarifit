 Uttvun 77, Tzayûr 2003 ( 2003) Tamazight Seg wezvru itnaba Yura t: Mmis n Nefusa (Libya, adrar n Nefusa) Seg wezvru itnaba Yelli mani tlaba Tasirt g taghmirt tella Yellis n tmura tefla Deffres tmagit tedja Azvettva tuli day tutva Seg wezvru itnaba Yelli mani tlaba Yelli djam swawal anfur Zzvun issvadv g umur Atvu n tagmudvi tuqejjur Fertven itran s upezur izri nnegh meqqer iteqqur. Seg wezvru itnaba Yelli mani tlaba Yelli ghllen tmazight am Tira g ijdi yessafwi Atvu atemda tamuli ifar ay iblilli.
