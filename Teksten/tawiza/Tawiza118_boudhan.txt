 tamssizwarut Lmuvrib, yat n tahrst d taorabt s tidtt (Le Maroc, véritable colonie arabe) yura t: mupmmd budhan yizzar nenna is lmuvrib itddar yat n tashrst (colonisation) d tadlsant, d ilsyant (linguistique) u d ijjdyant (religieux). Maca ommars (ursar) nenna is ntta itddar tashrst d tasrtit (politique). acku d imuvrabiyn i inbbän (gouverner) tamazirt n lmuvrib, waxxa (mqqar) tqqnen ixf nsn var waorabn. Maca tavawsa (affaire) n uvmis (journal) "nican" tsskan is lmuvrib d tahrst tääaffar timura n waorabn, maci di tutlayt (langue) d idls (culture) d ijjd (religion) waha, tääaffa itn di tsrtit s imant nns. d aorabn ad as itinin i inbbaän imuvrabiyn min iqqn ad ggn u min iqqn ur tggn. Inbbaän imuvrabiyn ssnsan (appliquer) umlan (instructions) i d asn d itææaän zi tmura n waorabn zund min itmsarn di tihras. Uyad ituman d dg isusln (ordres) i d ifka (yuca) ubarlaman (parlement) n lkawit i unabaä (gouvernement) amuvribi puma ad imarrt (au sens de "punir") ayt "nican". inbbaän imuvrabiyn slkmn (exécuter) assusl n lkawit s udraf (célérité), mqqar "nican" d yan n uvmis d amuvrabi, itadf di tvawsiwin n ugnsu (intérieur). Da tmarsan isqsan ad: wi inbbän lmuvrib? inbbaän nns imuvrabiyn niv ayt lkawit? is vars ca n uzarug (indépendance) d tanaya (souveraineté) nvd uhu? is tanbaät (pouvoir) tamuvrabit tqddc (servir) i tnafut (intérêt) n lmuvrib niv i tnni n lkawit? mani illa ulu (dignité) n imuvrabiyn mani tnbaät nnsn tsslkkam umlan d infurn (étrangers) tqqnen var yat n tvawsa n ugnsu? manaya (ayad) d tashrst d tamdadt (véritable) n waorabn i lmuvrib. yavul zund ca n tamnaät (province, région) n lkawit. ttsnas asusl n waorabn ad as d itasn zi "mitobul" (métropole) n ugmuä (orient) aorab zund tuva ttg fransa mani tuva tshrs (coloniser) lmuvrib. mamc vad nslullt (libérer) lmuvrib s tashrst taorabt? iqqn ad t nslullt amzwaru s inbbaän iorubiyn i t yarrin d tahrst n waorabn. u mamc vad nslullt lmuvrib s inbbaän iorubiyn? s usuvul n tmazivt dg ansa nsn. dix, aslullt n lmuvrib ad yili s tmazivt u i tmazivt.
