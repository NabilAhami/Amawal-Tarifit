 Uttvun 78, Mrayûr 2003 (Octobre 2003) Tamazight ? Da wen qqaregh yarut: Obdella Uhassi (Aghbala n ayet Ssexman) A win iftel i oari, a win ican ixef i lexla, a wi pat tucekam, aghulad s ansa, raoat ma ta kunni, ma ytelsam mani tekkam ma yteram ma yedda tenadam, da wen qqaregh a win mi yettel rebbi adu i ssexub assen afus d uqqemu awin nagh ikesen iles, awin agh d win yites, pan annli iyya ifri, ttehdu magh abu newwas d llmutanebbi, aragh turzzum aorq, tajjim tanmilat iyan larziqq. 2002 Tawiza. .
