 uïïun 107, krayur 2006 (Mars 2006) Tamazight Awal n usussem Yura t: Salah ngab (Libya) Redmun ighfawen saddu tamurt D imlun tregh s tafya nnes tufut D may wukzun senzun in tattut D si meghregh, ghry tittvawin wukzegh Imlun d: sussem d aknagh tsilut. Zvunnun tanezzaght, yutva zvettva Seghrun ara nnsen mammek terwla Imlygh asen: iytvaren rahu in tugurya Imlun d: sussem , d aknefk idrym D aknerni di tzvit nnegh tawtra. Amghar nnsen amawal yentvel D yemlasen af azetvaf rahu mellel D si imlygh asen: s tmura tanmmirt terwel Imlun d: sussem ad k nedj amezwar D imel n ara nnek may netcin nemma. Sestnun d irrun: awal nnek d tkerkas swa swa Irrygh ghefsen: iytci zerregh di tghawsa tella Wil imlunidc sussem, wil imlunidc cra Islyghten tetcawrun, imlun: imlut ahydug D islygh memmi yemmal may imlun flla. 2002 Tawiza. .
