 uïïun 107, krayur 2006 (Mars 2006) Tamazight Gujil n assnatv Yura t: Salah Ngab (Libya) Assnatv tamzva terna D si negh mendi oukrun Gujil yenghu baba Yetcu ayetmas illzvun. Yeddert assnatv assa Yekrez tamurt amdun yeqqer Yunir iysergh tlaba Gujil mi yizmir ayemgher. Ibrid yezzegret guda Yugur amatvu d yuded Gujil iysenz tmura Memmis assa mammek yemred. Yedderghel manis yetta Am wugrifew yedder imlun Mi yufa n taslit tawiza D iles n memmis iyzun. Tudkal nnes isaly n wujenna A rabbi surfiyyed yemlu Mi tezmir ataly tawtra D am babis memmis yenghu. 2002 Tawiza. .
