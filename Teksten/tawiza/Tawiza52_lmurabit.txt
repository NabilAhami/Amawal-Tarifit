 52, (Août 2001) Tamazight Akettuy n yan uselmad Yura t Abdennajim Lmurabit Jirbas (Mezzuj, Lhusima) d tarezzift i mas Oumar Ttvawes g Igulmimen) Iwerghi yi wudem, irgig wul inw, Ibbi digi wawal, iles inu yegnu. Iskwumdva y umettva ay gan isegnu, A yekniwen yezzvilen ur n akem nettu. Ma rad ittun bugafer idlen s amalu, Ayettu imazighen izdghen g usfalu, Idir idda s ssuq ulli tehdvuten t luhu Ar teqqel alkam n yewwis yeccu. Annayegh yut tudert temmezray xef Rrif inu, Tudert ighezzifen seg wakud n wezvru. Ay agurdv n tudeght a yisk amezwaru, Seg wass lli k zvrigh aghulegh gigh aswu. Taghi tghufi n Tinghir d Bumalen Dades, Ktighed imeddukal asag netmun g usays, Ggudin yexfawen ku yan issen asagh nnes. Ur nad ttugh tagulmimt ar tagh ghifes Id bab n izerfan ad wwin yyiles. Meqqar kun ssiwdven s tenghi d uturres, Isul umuttel asen inghel adif seg ighes, Ad aylalen itbiren aghilif ad ikkes, Ay ighrem ioezzan is das bnun askaw nnes? Tebbit tasa nu ittures wul ides, Ay arraw imazighen tamazight amzvat diges, Pan kra n tadvfin seg selmad gher mma nnes. (Mezzvuj,Llhoceima gi 98) 2002 Tawiza. .
