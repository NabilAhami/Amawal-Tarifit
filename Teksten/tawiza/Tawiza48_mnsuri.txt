 48, (Avril 2001) Tamazight Tamazight n ku yan Yura t: Mensvuri Hemmu (Ayt Ishaq Xnifra) Ddut da ad teddum, Zeyedat gher dat, Zeyedat gher dart. Amazigh ayjed yix, Ur nnix ad dt ttux, Tamezgha g ul inew, Abda n wabda. Meqqar tannim tinifest tesvemmidv, Lazafit tella. Tasafut nnex, Ad t nasi s ifassen nnex, Ad t nasi s ufus nnex. Amazigh a yemma, Tamazight a wetma, Ur nnin qqah ghi negh tesin, Mec nekwni nezzva afus g wacal. Nini s uqmu yuk: Tamazight nnex aya ur nenni at t nezeri, Tamazight nnex aya ursar t nettu Ayt Ishaq, 15/2/1994. 2002 Tawiza. .
