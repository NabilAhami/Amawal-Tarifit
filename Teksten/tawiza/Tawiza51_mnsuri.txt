 51, (Juillet 2001) Tamazight Da cem tirix ciyyan Yura t: Hemmu Lmensvuri (Xnifra) Ul nnem, anu axatar n fad, Deg amaray iga Timeqit yut. Isexsin bapra taghufi. Ul nnem, amalu asemmvad Deg ssentalex Fferex tiwirega Ad ur xelin t s irifi. Ul nnem amellal Am ussan ikkan Lli tela tudert, Tatvesva nes ijelan. Ul nnem aglman adal, Itri issiden I teyedjuyet Ikkan g ul inew tendagh Isulen altu tzedagh. Akkmani Ul nnem d tasarut Ittanafen Tiketin inu iqqan. Ttadji adix, zeg uzemz S azemez, Yat titpi zeg titpa N wul nnem. Ttadji x as yat tmulit Ad siresex aqecac Xef udmer nnem Zelax g tagganet Tazeddayet N wul nnem. Ttadji ad gga fix Ggezex Tagharect n wul nnem. Ad ferurix, Flufelex, Fsix, Jaj n il aziza N wul nnem. Ayt Ishaq, 11/4/2001 2002 Tawiza. .
