 uïïun 105, ynyur 2006 (Janvier 2006) Tamazight Assa Yura t: Salah Ngab (Libya) Assa sis iysagd nnegh D many nerwel yufa nnegh Tazla dis tsemdaya nnegh Selnu yeggu ifrewen d iyfar Nuri ibrid s tukza nnegh Ma ca netta selnu si yummut amghar. Assa yemgher flla nnegh Yatcar s test tudnen nnegh Tiwdi dis i yseghra nnegh D netta dis ammek anugur Ur neslet dytca iysewel flla nnegh Nuta di amermur deffer amermur. Assa di tamcaput iyseder nnegh Tidet di tsisawt iysetaya nnegh Di ifri amzvu yetca nnegh D welyuc am ibehlal netsugum di dytca Ur nukezt it tamedurt izzat temda. 2002 Tawiza. .
