 56, (Décembre 2001) Tamazight Tamacaput n imezvianen Tura t: Hasna Ayt Heddu (13 n iseggusa) Tedda tbibetv teqelluli g urarn, teles lizar. Tesugger s igenna, teg tazvulet. Tebezv afus g axurri, teles izebyan. Tserreh sellum, teles ikurbin. Tennejmeo d wuccen, inna s:«Ma tessekrek ig tufit ayennagh?». Toawd as iwiddagh tesseken. Iddu iqelluli g urar, yagh t uljdvidv. Isugger s igenna, ioema. Ibezv afus g uxarri teqqeset tgherdvmet. Iserreh sellum irezv. Tehsva t: Hesna Ayt Heddu, 13 n iseggusa Yaru t: Lhusin DMAMI. 2002 Tawiza. .
