 56, (Décembre 2001) Tamazight Ashinhen n izvewran Tura t: Mayssa Racida (Bentveyyeb, Nnadvûr) Marra gharyent tfuyin i d yenqaren Di tghuyyit n wussan, Deg ûreqqasen n zman, Di zpir n iraghan. Nuyur ticri yeggûren D ticri yetrajan... Msarwasen yibriden d wattvasv n ighezvran. Tariwin, idurar, tacediya deg iyran. Asmupart n isvemmidven d ushinhen n izvewran. Rami d neghmi ighir anegh d asennan Tiffray n wurtu wdvant i d d iseqsan. Rqabra d ttenni i ten issirden ghar imedvran. Tittvawin icten tru d, icten oad toayan Fulleoleo di remrah Tanuffra axmi d gh ad mgharen iseyman Ad tennufser tiri n ioerbab Tmmurt nnegh mmuten ayet bab... Mayssa Racida 2002 Tawiza. .
