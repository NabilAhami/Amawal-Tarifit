 55, ( 2001) Tamazight Taghucet n wul (chanson du cœur) Yura t: Mansvuri Hammu (Ayt Ishaq) Ar cem ssarax Kix akw tiberdin, D wadgharen isentlen. A tafuket izelan, Dared n tillas n iman. Ar ghifem rezzux, Bbix adraren d izughar, Isaffen imuoar. A tadvfi iddan, A tvadvsa ikkan. Ar cem tanadax, Zvrix isanfen n ujarif, Isacaten n wasif. A taydjuyt ifsan, S usidd d waman. Ar hettvux Iles inew ihlan, Ur titenala uberan. Amezruy iteqqisen, Ad ur iyecim advan. Tilelli ighudan, Ur ttisukef yan. Izerfan n umadan, Ad serirrent gher ku yan. Ar terajax Midden inew iguten, S yan igerdvan, Ad inin: «Nlula d imazighen ad nemmet d imazighen». A yul inew iyenghan, Ad ur tedzad ciyan. Ayt Ishaq, 22/9/2001. 2002 Tawiza. .
