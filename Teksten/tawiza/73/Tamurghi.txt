 Uttvun 73, Semyûr 2003 (Mai 2003) Tamazight Tamurghi (sauterelles) Yaru t: Muha Mellahni (Ayt Ishaq) Tedda d tmurghi yiwi t id uherraf, Day rehbex as. Nnix adj it ad tedder lliy t id izzeo bupeyyuf. Ur dmix id anedvlib n tzizut ayd teya, Tufa isekwla d ilemuten izizawen, D isaffen d ighebula ayet waman izeddayen. Da ar d tmessaraw ad c temjebar iy teoedda, Da yinegh lazv ayt tmaziret iresvlin. Hedvix c a yaneqqer nnix ad nesenna zi takka ayed tyam, Da ur dmix tenna d itekkan aghelluy Pa c tinna yac innan taresvlitn tamaziret ayd yix. Dar tsemxazzay ayt babin n wacal iredvlin Tjera yax tin bu temettvut d adverghal Ran ac yin a lexir iy pen ikkusa unna ur asen d ihemi rebbi. (Ayt Ishaq, 11 2002) 2002 Tawiza. .
