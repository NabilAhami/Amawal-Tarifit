 Uttvun 73, Semyûr 2003 (Mai 2003) Tamazight Issi d tissit Yaru t: Ihya Muhemmed (Ayt Ishaq) Illa yissi, tili tissit. Mec d tisit tadnubet ayd teyya. Lliy teqqen imi ifri. Tefukka amazan n Sidi Rebbi, Zi ayt Qurayc igheddvar, Lliy ran ad zlin i Lislam anetraq. Uma issi bu weobudv azvurar, Izzenzan ssem, tifighra tadverghalet ur da tessidd a awed yuk Unna mi teqqes asint s isvendval Meqqar illa akw wanecti Nekkin zund inesi ayed yix Da swalax tadawet inew mmi sennan I unna ur iyehemill iy qqimix abda i temaggit nnay kkix. 2002 Tawiza. .
