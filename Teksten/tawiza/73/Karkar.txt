 Uttvun 73, Semyûr 2003 (Mai 2003) Tamazight Yedjis n dcar inu Yura t: Karkar Wajed (Midvar, Nnadvûr) Yedjis n dcar inu, fpem awar inu, Ta d thajit inu ze ûmi tedjid d gheddu. War zeg iwdan ura zeg wi djan inu Teddaregh d ametluo iweddvar array inu. Iweddvar ayi webrid maca di dcar inu, Nec day s d rbarrani icarp ayi reodu, Icarp ayi maghar nec arezzugh tasghart inu. Yedjis n dcar inu fpem awar inu, Cem i dayi yeqqimen zi tudart inu Ad tagh d cem i ghad ygharen thajit inu, Cem i trajigh ad tfepmed awar inu. 2002 Tawiza. .
