 uïïun 100, tamyur 2005 (Août 2005) Tamazight Asif izwan Yura t: kujut hassan (warzazat) ingid xasif bbin iberdan llix g tigzirt, ger imedwan grex aghuri lah maydi ysellan yiwy actal, yiwy ikerwan igad tileghmin, issentel ileghman ihufd s unzvar, idel igherman ingha awessar, ingha amezzan iwet bu wghejdim, irdel unna ibeddan medden igellin ahclaf aydgan medden igellin, gan iwetvfan argaz awessar, ula wadda d ilulan nsan agensu n umda ighban zund igwra mid iselman grex titv s tafuyt d itran lah isignew, lawla usman ihma wzal, iga irafan ddupdix, ur ssinx mayejran awyid kan ineghmisen ayamazan.
