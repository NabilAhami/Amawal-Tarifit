 uïïun 103, ymrayur 2005 ( 2005) Tamazight sifaw Yura t: Mmis n tmurt (Libya) Izekwan itcarun, itcarun s wulawen n wudan D welyuc iddrun, welyuc di ighleden illan A tagherma n iderghalen, islut tsisawt di tsisawen illan Si nsugum ansil tamchut, ur nislu d netta awal nnegh kan Nennetv in nnaj af tufut, si in adday wutan itran Tuden tusna nnsen tattut, am irgazen am tsednan Ibrid yetcar s taqut, m nukez many anary teklywan Redmunt it s tiwdi tanut, ur nerwa si nessu sis aman Agar idgha gh tuffegh tamedrarut, d tamdit wil tefkuc imatkan Imlun a taburt n arwas arut, negh sistnut mammu di amekdan Lemmy sifaw yummut, d limy ass netvlun iggun islan Yuri tukza nnes n ara n tamurt, ur wukzunt mammu itant wudan Netvlun d welyuc tssal dis tamdurt, yedder di wujenna itry s itran. 2002 Tawiza. .
