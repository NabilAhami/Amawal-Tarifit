 Uttvun 62, Sedyûr 2002 (Juin 2002) Tamazight Tamazight Yaru t: Uoettva (Tinghir) Tamazight, a yaldjig n tefsut, Ikka ghif m uzerdab n unebdu, D ugris n tegrest, D izvuten n lexrif, Tesquzzit ur terzit ula teknit! Tamazight, a tizwa n ughulid, Ssumman am ighuran Skumdven am urtan, Ur am udjin awed izvuran! Tarut tamment, tagit a teqqart! Tamazight, a timilla n talwit, Tudjit asen amalu ddaw useklu nem. Akren tiglay g ugatuf nem Tirirt asen izlan n umarg s nniyt Ar xerrdven nitni ad bbin tassaft! Teqqimt teggalt asen ur ttefghet! Tamazight, a yaghbalu n wamas n ulmu, Teswa dig m tesut s tesuta, Ksen dig m fad inejda, Tked asen mayd igan ka Irin ad kem ngvren icenga! Ggadin waman tagit, a tasmunt! Tamazight, a tisent n tudert inew, A yizvli n uwessar, A yazmumeg n tegzviwet, A tadvsva n umezzan. Ur ssinegh walu meg ur kem ghrigh Ur ssinegh tayri, ur kem righ.
