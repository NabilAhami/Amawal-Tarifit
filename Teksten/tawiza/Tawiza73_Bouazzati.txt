 Uttvun 73, Semyûr 2003 (Mai 2003) Tamazight Min ghad yinigh? Yura t: Buoezzati Muhsin (Nnadvûr) Min ghad ynigh i tudert Nettat d anegh yessrun, Min ghad ynigh i zman, Yizwaren war kd negh imun, Min ghad ynigh i wseggûs a, D iseggusa yeodun Min ghad ynigh i bettvu War d anegh yejjin bu netmun. Min ghad ynigh i tadjest, I yeffaren tidett teghz as s adu wqedduh, I yessazigen azvri n tudert Zegga tughat ifuh Min ghad ynigh iw metluo Yarewren yuyaren isuh Ma sinigh arah d Ma sinigh svafi ruh. Min ghad ynigh i reodu Yettun ward anegh yessin Ma sninigh axarriq Yeqqim oad war nnin Ad t’ sqasegh zi rehriq, Inetqasa neccin, Ad sucegh timessi, Ad yessen mayn din. 2002 Tawiza. .
