 Uttvun 71, Krayûr 2003 (Mars 2003) Tamazight Tudert i tmazight Yaru t: Abu Oali Oebdeoaziz (Merrakec) Tudert i tmazight, azul i ymazighen. Rebbi awen yan isigiln yan itlin. S ufulki nta d lepna inagh lan Rebbi awen ya ghila s loar n tmazirt ns ula awal ns. Wali irdan s tmazirt ns irdva s wawal ns. Wanna i yran Tamazight ur ad tnt ifal Ura ysmeh gh wawal n dars ira t isawal. Irrba tarwa f tmazight ad tnt ur tun. Ula lherf ns, tifinagh ad tnt ur tun. Timazighin d imazighen sawalat awal nun. Iryat Tamazight ad tiara s lherf ns. Lherf ns tifinagh ad t isn ku yan. Azul a ymazighen, tudert i tmazight Yan iran Tamazight igh iga amazigh Agis iylis s loar ad tnt ur ifal. 2002 Tawiza. .
