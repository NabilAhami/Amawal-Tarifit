 Uttvun 71, Krayûr 2003 (Mars 2003) Tamazight Imazighen n imal Yarut: Aghbalu Oaziz (Ayt ishaq) Paya cen uoeban, Ama ggan akw amazigh Yat afus g ufusi Kud tsvan inedvliben(1) diyi. Tbenum ax d axxam. Tyim as tiresal(2) svehnin, Ur ireddel meqqar(3) tid isvud uzwu n uharraf. Muat qah tseoeddum ax d timesmunin, Bar ad nessiwedv aggwa ad izzvayen ghif i. Amm adday tebdvum, Amm yan iyyis sezvanin; Ur iteywaqqad, Ur as netegga tareku(4) n ughanim. Wax as munat teyimed Yan uqemu gher s awal, Bar ad t yim izem issexlaoen akw lghaci. Pay i da ad wen qqarex a ymazighen, Ar sghuyyux. Bar is ur tsellam i weya da awen ttinix. Id is ur tpewwilem s uy lli nna awen ittuyan, Ma d is ur terdvim Tamazight ad t y tinnum. Wa pat n sghusen idellisen nnex imezwura, Apa bremmen amezruy nnex amezwaru. Pat nnan ax imazighen imezwura: «Unna ur irurin ahezzaz(5), Asenus bba tsen» Wa pa n imal tunef oad aqemu nes ku yass, A wa da teoayan ca n imazighen iresveliyen. Wa pa c Hemmu aselway nes amezwaru, Rix ad yin amm uhemmar nes ur ttatuy. Rix Iheya d Ikku Xamis amm uweqqaf nes. Rix ad ur ttuhilen Oawwen ax awed anezwum nes. Iwa pa n imal aseggûs nes. Rix ann afex oad Imal tiwedv iqerin nes awi. Ayt Ishaq, 22/1/2003 Amawal: 1 - Ennemi, 2- Piliers, 3 - Même si…, 4 - Emplâtre, 5 - Vengeance. 2002 Tawiza. .
