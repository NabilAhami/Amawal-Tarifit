 Uttvun 72, Kuzvyûr 2003 (Avril 2003) Tamazight Hezzmet oawt hezzmet Yaru t: Idir Zekkuri (Meknas) Hzzem toawt hzzemt a gma amedyaz, A wadda ihdvan awal nnes Ar t issiwidv i tsutiwin, Ar as n ttini t g timedyazin nnek, Tifinagh d asekkil n wawal nnek, Tinna ad gh d zrin imezwura nnegh. Mec thzzemt ussan ghif wiydv Ar tssarat idgharen ghif wiydv Ar tsawalt i medden Ghif tmaggit nnek D wawal nnek d usekkil nnes. Pat yiwdv lhal a toawt a thzzemt Ussan toawt asen idvan Mec trit ad yughul Umur i wawal nnegh, Imcidda t toawat g tmedyazin nnek. Hzzem toawt thzzemt a Mma, Mec tsvbert i yghetran, Ghif tfeqqah n iodawn, Iran ad agh kksen awal nnegh. Zlin timitar nnes, Is sghusen kullu idlisen, Yarun s tmazight Ula winna ghifs i sawalen Tsvbert kem turut tamazight G udem nnem d ifassen nnem S tiyzzay ur itffeghen, Aqmu nnem ar t tmmet Turut t g ihendiren Tpeyyut g usettva Af ad agh di tgulu Rexxu ixesvsva ad toawt a tsvbert Negh a toawt a txbert udem nnem Macan seg mayd tmhhent ssvahet nnem Nek pat ghiygh ad kegh idammen inu Ghir oawd hzzem tsvbert kidi. (tsul...) 2002 Tawiza. .
