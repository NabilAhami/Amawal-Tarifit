 43, ( 2000) Tamazight Tiseghnest Tura t: Yemma s n Yuba Tiseghnest d tamedyazt Ad t arigh s uoeffar. Ad t ighennej unitci At t ighvar umehdvar. D anaruz n useymi zi Tlalit ar gh ad yemghar. D tagrawla n umazigh ass, I di yuri ghar wedrar. D tiwsi zi ghar Nunja d Dipya wami tugha teddar. Tennam arem zzay s a yabehrûr Ghnes zzay s a rizar. Ghens it x idmaren d Tracna i tmazight ad teddar. D awessvi i cem a yelli Arecm it x izughar. Isem nnem d amazigh Rehm as ghar m ad m iweddvar. Seqsa amezruy x Yughurtha d Yuba min tugha yeqqar: Tamazgha, Tamazgha tudert d Ay m d asitem i wadvan d asafar. Tugherma nnem d izvewran n umadval d Tidett war dejji min gh ad neffar. 2002 Tawiza. .
