 Uttvun 76, Tamyûr 2003 (Août 2003) Tamazight S wuddur )D tarezzift i Numidya n Umupad n Dades d takat n Ucerri( Yaru t: Uoeqqa (Amur n yakuc( Ur idd ig kem-issentel umedlu, idd is ur tellit Nghedd is ismar umareg nnem g wul inu, mek festigh Ur uminegh, ig i-nnan winna kem ur irin ur tehlit Ur uminegh amezruy unsvib ig i-yenna wer jin tt-nn-kkigh? Seqsayat idellisen dda nara, d umareg dda nenna D mecta yad ghifun nseqsa, d mecta ya nulla. Iseggwasen d tsutiwin ad ghifnnegh izrin d tmara, Uten-agh, ssiwdven-agh ig kem ur nettu yabda, Beddlen-agh ilesawan, qqnen-agh imawen, nnan nfesta, Ur ssinen tayr i nnem, idd izveghwran d wul inew ag tella, Ur ssinen ayenna ddregh is kem ur ttugh ara. Mek idd amedlu ad agh-ibdvan, yat tezwutt tekkes-ta Mek idd tazakart nna s kem-kerfen, Kratv itvudvan, qenna ad ts-bbin, raren-kem-ida, Ig ghalen is am-udren, pan imdvel win ikweccadv ayg AMAWAL: Amezruy unsvib: l’histoire officielle - Tisutiwin: les générations -Tazwutt: le vent - Kref: ligoter -Imdvel: la tombe 2002 Tawiza. .
