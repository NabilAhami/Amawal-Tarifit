 uïïun 107, krayur 2006 (Mars 2006) Tamazight Aderghal Yura t: Salah Ngab (libya) Aderghal yuded af tawurt Yedrez tawurt Teslu tamurt Iysiwel af mammu asetyar Imlu nes: ikem aktwu temmar Yemlu: pad sekmegh di tufut. Aderghal yekmu tawurt teqqes Imlun nes Tufut in gaj mi nghes M nghes nezer many nella Nghes ad nedder yemerz zettva. aderghal yuded niten oeddun yukur wugurun d si yenced mi irrun d necdun may temmmaled netcin mi nessal nessen may yemmeml s awal d dis nemmal d yezvru ighed n wara, dis amatvu n tusna serghun Aderghal yemlu: cekwen d netci swa Ghernegh tetvawin maca Mi nzver di cra Wuddet af tzvit D inghut si wen taslit Cekwen am mammu yedder di zekka. 2002 Tawiza. .
