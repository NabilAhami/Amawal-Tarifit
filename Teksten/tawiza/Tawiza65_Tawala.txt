 Uttvun 65, Tzayûr 2002 ( 2002) Tamazight Tawala Yaru t: Ihya Muhammed (Ayt Ishaq) A yafellah, Ker bermed aman Tiwidv ci d tawal. Ssu urti ad ker igjugen. Uma Tawala n tenbadvt Ur ten annay. Bdvan t warraw Ur ad ax ssamun. A tasafut, Rix ad cm nasi, Ad nessid Abrid gher imal Nkes isnnan, Nssu igjugen Afad ad tezdegh Tawala Llin inamur Addur Jaj n tamazirt nna ten yuran. Amawal: 1 - tawal: l'alternance. 2 - imal: le futur. 3 - tanbadvt: le pouvoir. 4 - isnnan: les épines. 5 - inamur: pluriel de 'anamur' qui signifie 'citoyen'. 6 - addur: le rôle. 2002 Tawiza. .
