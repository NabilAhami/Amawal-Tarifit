 uïïun 134, sdyur 2958 (Juin 2008) Tamazight Asekelu Yura t: Ali Amaniss Awal iga ghur nnegh am usekelu, Iqqen n ad t tezzvu, neg as aman, Ad t nawey ar tama n ughebalu, Iga yasen zund aghebalu i yiman. Ad as nekkes g ddaw as asellebu, Afad yan wass ay iwet s uzvegher, Ul nnes ay ig jjemmar, ad ur ibu, Ad as iddu yigheneka ad ur inedder. Awal nnegh amazigh ayd yad gan, Unna t iran, niri t, awd ar as nekessa, Unna t ur irin, ul unegal ayd as gan, Aman irewan, unnagh ur da ten issa. Awal amazigh isul ard yagh tudert, Ad as ig aleddejig amellal g uqqemu, Afad ad sar ur yisin fad, iwet s tgedert, Azvegher g waman, ifer iga yalemu. Awal amazigh iga ghur i am usekelu, Llan as waman ammas n izvegheran, Ifer ikka yigenna, ur igi yameddu, Isul ard agh ik tiyeni, arwen ighweran. 2002 Tawiza. .
