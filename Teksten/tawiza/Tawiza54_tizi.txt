 54, (Octobre 2001) Tamazight Tizi n Taqqa Nureddin Bawdra (Gulmima) Tasga nnagh tekkes, D agensu ns inecfen; Dat usaddi n tafyut. Yan ighalen kra nnidven Yasey d ar iseksiw, Mad yettyarun ghef Tseggwin n ugens inecfen. Agensu nna di tessafegh Tizi n Taqqa, tasga.. Inurzvem, s tdusi, g usaddi… Tirra, i wen, wer ntilent. Max?! Pat nitni ayd iran Ugin salen ad agin Ar ttinin digh upu, Ar rettun ad yaccek Wnaghiz nk ghef wakal… Tizi n Taqqa tekkerfes Tennal amerret tsew tamessi. Ayen tghald is tesbeddid Ass agi serredlen t kel Tizi. Tasga nnagh tekkes d Agensu nnes inecfen Dat wen n tafuyt. Tit wersar tentil Ttat nitni ayd iran Ar ttinin digh ala! Upu… upu… wapi… Ar rettun ayt Tizi Ad yaccek unaghiz nk Ghef wakal. Irrfel uyen tesbedded. Kel Tizi n Taqqa siwlen Is tsellad i wapi nsen?… 2002 Tawiza. .
