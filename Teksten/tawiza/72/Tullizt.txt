 Uttvun 72, Kuzvyûr 2003 (Avril 2003) Tamazight Tullizt (Récit) Takujdlla tar itersiwen Yaru t: Dilai Essalih (Ayt Ishaq) Yan wass, kkix d ttverf n Tumelilin. Oettvrex n, iy tentel tafuyet. Feghex d zeg ureti tattuy test. G ubrid, bbix gher yat taddaret, ad did nesex ar tifawet. Rehben ayi id bab nes. Oemren d atay. Netta ar nettemsaw, raoax as i bab n txamet gher idvaren, afex pen win tefunast. Kkerx zeg uteci, nnix asen rix ad yenex. Imun d id i gher anesa di gganex. Rulex, kkex asefaylu (fenêtre). Yuci d id i, iyeri g terwla, iy d iwdvex ifran n ugidu (sable). Wetetx kecmex yuk disen. Cedvex ar tekrussuyex. Umzvex gher yan ughulid. Pezzax en zar imi n ifri, annayex tan ureyaz nna mi t' ttveona t'iddi, mi wesoen ighariwen. Inesa azennar amellal. Yuzved id aceddvur. Azvdvex afus afad ad t' amzvex, yakkuf d uzvru. Ar tekujdellex, aqqax n abudv nes. Xaylex yan ifili n usidd. Ttujix ad raoax matta netta. Sellax ca n wawal. Suggrex, afex n ibba d imam da fettvren! 2002 Tawiza. .
