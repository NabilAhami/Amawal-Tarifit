 Uttvun 72, Kuzvyûr 2003 (Avril 2003) Tamazight TADVA Yaru t: Oeqqa Pat icerreg wul inu mek am iga, Udem inu s imettvawen isemmeghi d tuga. Anezgum nnem usigh t urta d luligh Zayd nn azemmul i wayyatv a tasa Tgit asammer nnegh nqubl kem apa Icenga nnegh ar d zarnnegh ttebbin asanf Amm ifigher da kkaten kecmen s ighisa Kerfen kem s tzakart seg tfednin ar akyutv Ulawen nnegh s ughurru gwzan t aytma S imikk n dvuru awal nnegh zzenzan t Kan asen tazart ig ten oelfen Tawda tkwerf asen idvaren. Utvun d zarnnegh ccwun d iqwerray Zzvan dignnegh iskawen ilkem agh adif Mek urmegh tawada rrezzven i yiwerzan. Is urmegh awal rrezzven i uxwsan Sulen dignnegh idammen ad ur tat Qad am hrigh imettvawen tsult ad ttvessat Ayenna tsul TADVA tudert nnem ur tesmar. (Oeqqa 11\02\2953 g Wamur-N-Yakuc) AMAWAL: Cerreg: dechirer - Tuga: l'herbe - anezgum: Le souci - Azemmul: cicatrice - Asammer: lieu ensoleillé - cengu: l'ennemi- asanf: raccourci- Amm: comme- Ifigher: serpent- Ighisa: les trou -Aghurru: la trahison - Gzi: tatouer - Tazart: la figue -Tawda: la peur -Iqwerray: les cornes -Iwerzan: les talons - Uxwsan: les dents. 2002 Tawiza. .
