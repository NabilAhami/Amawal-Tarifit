 Uttvun 72, Kuzvyûr 2003 (Avril 2003) Tamazight Atbir Imazighen ay tgid a Tawiza Yarut: Muhammed Peddawi (Oin Lluh) Mghar llan id oari ilint Tiza, Lla tawi d tibratin ger ayt Mazigh, Yaghul umazigh day ttisin Amazigh, A mmis Imazighen inu da tsalex digun. Is nega taymat is gix zzigun? Annig llix akwen ctix toezzem cigan, Ad ac rtux agma asggwas ighudan, Ad ac issudda wdvar ad tasi tagut, Toawendi uccen ad as odilex tazut. Patin xlani ycerwan icemmed digi, Smughin ul inw ssahel aoggadi. (Amazigh nTifsa, Oin Lluh) 2002 Tawiza. .
