 Uttvun 72, Kuzvyûr 2003 (Avril 2003) Tamazight Imuray Yaru t: Mellehni Muhammed (Ayt Ishaq) Unna neghan ayd yix, Da ttegwdex amarey ad i yyin d ahellal, A tadvefi iy da c dveffurex a yasmun. Max unna da yessarsa ca is llan oelaxir? A mmis n tehyudvt, a yanefnaf issaran g zzenaqi, Awa yat ca n uoeban i yixf nnun Aya d ur awen d yusi Mecta n yuk ayed ikkan ar issara Itewaoer as ixef iniyer imejjan ur t' fukkin A yahyudv, a yayed ad c nnix y en dat ac. Xas tenna issezwighen udem nes day tveferred fir as Is ur tannayed mayd ijeran menid ac Mecta n yuk ayd izerin arraw da ur da pen icetti Zzenzan timizar d igherman ur asen d yusi Ma c itteqbal a yawijil ur t' iyyin Iwa tter i rebbi bar ad iwen ihennq Uma laqiset ic ur da teywaqqas xas g imejjan. 2002 Tawiza. .
