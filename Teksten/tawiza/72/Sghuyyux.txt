 Uttvun 72, Kuzvyûr 2003 (Avril 2003) Tamazight …. Seghuyyux… Yarut t: Aghbalu Oaziz (Ayt Ishaq) Wida seghuyyux a imazighen aflla nnun Bar is tepwam awi ma dur ghurun ulawen, iy teyam amm iselli n ubrid ku yass, Xas ddabet nna kwen id ikkan ar c id itesmennaqar, Wa xas beddat ar d kwen id rezun gher axam, Apa teyim asen aneftil ar d merghghen acal, Wa xas mmuterat d a imazighen ad neoawen. Wida tebubbax anezwum nes ighelyi s wul Wa tega tmazight amm rrvay n ca n usafu Ur ixesi ur ttagh oemun ax d imettvawen. Cwi sellax i ca wetten tawuret ar seghuyyun Nnan ax fafat' tekrem d zeg idves pat neghan ax iy d beddix lehed iyya x win ughanim iy n pezzax titv inew amm idv deg lulix. (Ayt Ishaq, 24/1/2003) 2002 Tawiza. .
