 uïïun 132 kuçyur 2958 (Avril 2008) Tamazight Tarwa n tuccact Yura t: Said belgharbi Tezvwa d ghar negh tayyut, trehhef i dcar s ibehrar n rizar nnes acemrar. Rwazna tesghuyyu, ttemzawar, ttettar anuri i ymezdagh ijjin ibriden nnurezvmen i twasit n tarwa n tuccact. Ura d ijjen ma yarra s taynit i wjeojeo nnes. Tarwa n tuccact mermi mma tus d tmeddit ad nnufesren zeg ifran nsen, textutturen s rhess jar tisedjya d ibuda n dcar ghar iyennaren n imezdagh. Pa Rwazna icci t wur nnes, tesghuyyu x min tetten tarwa n tuccact zi tseqqar n imezdagh, ayennar nnes i twacar amen yeccur s iyazvidven, ayennar jjin t d aoaryan, ksen aoban i ssar nnegh tugha day negh yughin. Imezdagh twacemten di rmar nsen yeggin tadukli aked yifisen d uccanen d uccacen yeffuden tisessi n idammen. Tarwa n tuccact usin d ad anegh ccen, maca mayemmi ghar negh i d tasen wapa, mayemmi nejja iymaren nnegh arezvmen i weodawen nnegh? “Uyaret ad nendi, huma ad neymar marra yenni d anegh i tetten tasghart nnegh, faryet iymaren nwem, gget anday d asentil i dcar nnegh, huma war d tidfen tarwa n tuccact ghar uraq nnegh”. Tenna sen Rwazna i yemshessa n dcar, i yenni umi i twacar dcar. (Said BELGHARBI, Amazigh31@hotmail.com) 2002 Tawiza. .
