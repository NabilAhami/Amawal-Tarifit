 uïïun 132 kuçyur 2958 (Avril 2008) Tamazight Awal n yiman Yaru t: Ali Amaniss Ur illi ighimi ard yili wawal, Ur telli tghessa mi ur illi utvar, Ur illi telli tudert n bela yawal, Ur illi yiman xes ig illa utvar. Iqqen n ad nezd awal ard ineghed, Ad as neg ikiker afad ay yili atvu, Ad is neg aherir, igheneka ay ized, Afad yan wass ay iggidey, ay ibetvu. Awal irewan iga i wul am usafar, Da yas yikki atvu ard ig am amellal, Da yas ittekkes may ixxan d wafar, Afad ad dig s neg atebir d umelal. Ku yan yasey ad yuru awal axatar, Ad t ig g ittesen warraten ixataren, Ad dig s yuru amzeruy g unerar, Ig dig s kigan n iwaliwen imellalen. Awal amazigh irewan ghur i kigan, Righ ad t gegh g wul innew abeda, Afad ad as ikkes asekkin aseggan, Yan wass, ad d ilal, tekkes tbareda. 2002 Tawiza. .
