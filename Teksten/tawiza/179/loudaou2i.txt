 uïïun 179, krayur 2962 (Mars 2012) Tamazight Azul, A Ludawi Yura t: Hha Oudadess Â Tabesbast, Â Ghellil, Â Tinghir! Mad cemmin, Â Mmugadir, is tessent? Manza Ludawi? Manza, Bab n awal, Bab ne tassînit? Adêbib, aqusas, amgharê n ammegraw; Tawada ne amnay, Â unna ur t issinen. Nnan temmuttit, ur nedmi; Ku yan, abhâdê yusit. Awal yudef amezzugh, Adday t tennit; Asiwel n amesnaw, Ul ayd as issan as; idla as. Adgharê nnec imqqur; Mayd ids tegam, Â tajda d imeddukkal? Nessen, tudert is tella, Tili tamttant; Nnig asent, Akkuc, waryuger. Adêbib, aqusas, Win amudîn, iwdanen; Yumezê g tagit lli, Ne Hypocrate. Ur djin asen inni: Mayd tmest, Umani, Mayd takkat? Inna asen, i ku yan: Ini iyi, Mayd ce yaghen? Â Ludawi! Ssiwedê azul, I Dadda Âettâ, de akkw Mazigh. I akkw, dis, midden zîlnin. Hâ ac, Mnid awen, Abehri, Azayku; de mennawt wiyyadê. Munat, Smunat agraw meqqorn; Ar ak nessirim, Aghellay inn, Tudert ad ac timim. (Hha Oudadess, Rrebadê) 2002 Tawiza. .
