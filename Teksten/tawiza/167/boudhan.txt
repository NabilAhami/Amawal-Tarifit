 uïïun 167, krayur 2961 (Mars 2011) Tamazight tamssizwarut: tamukrist n tnttit di lmrruk (tafult tanggarut 4) (La problématique de l'identité au Maroc - 4ème et dernière partie) yura t: mupmmd budhan tanttit ingr tamazivt d tamuvribit: llan inni (Willià) italsn aliä iqqn ad nini is nkni d "imuvrabiyn" waha u maci d imazivn ula d aorabn. tuva nzmmr ad nddag (accepter) asufv ad, mri (mc, iv) azwl (nom) "lmuvrib" issummak (signifier) yat n tnttit n tmurt tamazivt, d tangmaät (spécifique) u d tamzrigt (indépendant) xf tnttit taorabt, zund azwl n iran issummakn yat n tnttit d tanagma^ät illan d tafarisit. maca iv "lmurib" ixs ad yini tanttit tamazivt, iri iffv d zg ugraw n waorabn illan d agraw n iwunak itmunan di tnttit nsn taorabt. dix, awal ad ittinin is iqqn ad igza (suffire) ad nili d imuvrabiyn ukan u maci d imazivn ula d aorabn, itssiswy (zg "iswi",but, = viser, fixer comme but) asuttl (siège) n tnttit tamazivt afad ad tsul tnttit taorabt tvmmar (dominante). oawd azwl "lmuvrib" ur itqqn qao vur tmurt tamazivt ula tutlayt tamazivt ula avrf amaziv. s ungma^ä, azwl ad d anfur (étranger) xf tmurt tamazivt acku inni t isqdcn d infurn xf tmurt tamazivt n lmrruk. azwl zi ituva itwassn lmrruk ntta d "murrakuc" illan d awwal d amaziv acku itqqn vur tmurt tamazivt. fransa d nttat i yksn azwl n murrakuc u tgga dg unsa nns "lmuvrib" puma ad tsmskl (tssnfl) tanttit tamazivt n lmrruk vur yat n tnttit d taorabt. tanttit d ijjd ntsla u nqqar^ i waïïas ttinin is "lislam ntta d tanttit nnv u maci d tmazivt ula d taorabt". is ijjd (religion) n lislam izmmar s tidtt ad yili d tanttit n inflasn (croyants) nns? yizzar nssgza (nssfru = expliquer) is tanttit d tulmist (spécificité) i yt^çlayn (distinguer) yan n uvrf xf qao ivrfan n umaäal. is lislam ntta d yat n tulmist ttçlay imrrukiyn xf qao ivrfan yaänin? uhu, acku lislam ur illi d ijjd n imrrukiyn waha, ntta d ijjd n ujar amlyar n inflasn dg qao umaäal. dix, ntta ur izmmar ad yili d tanttit, acku tnttit d min ibïïan jar ivrfan, uma lislam ntta d min ismunan jar qao n ivrfan imslmn. tanttit d uwank imala d tammurt i ytgucculn tanttit n uvrf itaddarn di tmurt ad, mamc yizzar nenna u nssfru, u imala tammurt d tafada (condition) tamzwarut i tilawt n uwank, agmuä is tanttit n uwank tddaffaë tanttit n tmurt mani inbbä uwank ad. s unalkam ur izmmar ad tili tnttit n uwanm ttmmçlay (être diffétrent) xf tnttit n tmurt. dix, tanttit n uwank d tnttit n tmurt nns d yat ur tmsbäint vur sin. asmi vad nnsn, dix, is tanttit n uwank d nttat d tanttit n tmurt s imant nns, ad nnssn agul (erreur) dg iwïïa umussu amaziv mani ur itettar asmzv n uwank aorab amrruki afad ad imsasa dg tnttit nns akd tnttit n tmurt nns tamazivt. tingmiyin (revendications) timazivin n umussu amaziv tvimant lbda dg uswir adlsan u oad ur iwänt vur uswir asrty (politique). aswir adisrty itgnses dg tngmit n uwank amaziv acku tamazirt n lmrruk d yat n tmazirt d tamazivt. 2002 Tawiza. .
