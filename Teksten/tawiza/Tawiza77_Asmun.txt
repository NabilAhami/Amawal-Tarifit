 Uttvun 77, Tzayûr 2003 ( 2003) Tamazight Asmun n yidv Yaru t: Ihya Muhememd (Tamsmunt n Imal, Ayt Ishaq) Am anna iwalf ku ammas n yidv, irezvem i ydvar nes ar iteddu, aneli nes ur illi dis. Ar itxemmam, ar itewarya. Yumezv amsaray s taghzi, ichid axub nna g lla, tuser tsuggerd ghif s, yufan ixef nes ur ili awed imiq, ur yiwil, walu tawuri, walu imal, izayd ar iteddu, ur issin is ar iteddu. Inezgh t ubrid d uxemmem iy ikcem g yan n teqzidrt (tamatart n uzray), ifafa zi tewargit nes, isugger gher teqzirt. Ur yumin en nes, ar pent imerri. Ighal is isul ar itewarya. May d yannay? Yannay ca, mat uya? Ur iqqim da ittakz walu. Igger aghuyyi: tifin… ur as yin ad icemmel awal, ur tudjin ad ifereh. Smughghin as ul. Yran is gher dvart. Usin d ignzan d tesmmarin, ar teqellaoen tiqzdirin. Ur qen ykiz macan issen mata nipni. Irzvem i wattvas n iseqsiten ingras d ixef nes. Ihil iy inyuddu, ikcem gher tewargit nes. 2002 Tawiza. .
