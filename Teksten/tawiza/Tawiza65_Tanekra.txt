 Uttvun 65, Tzayûr 2002 ( 2002) Tamazight Tanekra Yura t: Benoacur Zapir (Beggayt, Dzayer) Ghlin warrac d ilemziyen, mmaren idammen ghef tlelli d izerfan. Yyan seg lbattvel i - iddren ulawen gher daxel wwan. Manci d tiselbi i - yselben negh d tamurt ikerpen bghan tudert yehlan. Achal yeghlin d irgazen d ttlawin yegglen ghef tidett akkw ad tban. Yesberber fellas uwaghzen, ites deg dammen, yebgha ad yeqleo izvuran. Ass a msafaqen medden, kkren am yiwen, yerna zyes ttgan tura mi uzlen idammen, d tilelli ara maggren tamurt aslhun wusan. (Ass n 18/04/2002) 2002 Tawiza. .
