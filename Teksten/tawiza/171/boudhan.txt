 uïïun 171, sayur 2961 (Juillet 2011) Tamazight tamssizwarut: iãiä n tamazivufubit d tipuäyt n taoarubamanya (rage de l'amazighophobie et folie de l'arabomanie) yura t: mupmmd budhan zg ami issiwl ugllid mupammd wis säis xf asmnäw n tmazivt, ssntan imazivufubiyn dg twagit nsn n usnãb n tutlayt tamazivt. uma umi d ffvn invmisn ttinin is tmazivt tavul d tunãibt di tmnäawt tamaynut, imazivufubiyn yucar itn wul, ggurn ad ppuäyn u ad asn tffv tvçint (raison). afad ad sbddn asnãb n tmazivt, ssufvn d qao arbab (alaf =arme) nsn puma ad wtn di tmazivt. ilafn ad tgnsisn dg isrkasn d umyiyn (mythes). alsn ma illan dg isrks axatar n "ddahir abarbari" illan d asrks amqran n umzruy n lmrruk. dix, qao "inçatn" nsn d isrkisn acku bddn xf usrks axatar n "ddahir abarbari". imazivufubiyn ad, dig sn ula d inni iccatn di tmazivt zg ism n lislam. maca is asnãb n tmazivt itmyaba akd lislam? zmmrn ad anv fkan yan n unça waha iskan d is lislam ntta mgal asnãb n tmazivt? di tidtt, nitni maci lislam i d asn icqan. zg ism n waçur aoarab i tagin asnãb n tmazivt. u mani tarran taynit i waçur, ta^ååajn xf lislam u tadsn vur ljahiliya. d wad d amnzay nsn, tajahilit mani tuva ^açur vur s azal d amqran. asmi vad nssfzy addat n imazivufubiyn, ad naf is nzzvn xf tfransist u maci xf taorabt. max? acku taorabt ur tzmmar ad arnu tafransist maynzi nttat ur tlli d yat n tutlayt itwasqdacn dg umsawaä zund tafransist. dix, tutlayt izmmarn ad tarnu tafransist d tamazivy illan ula d nttat d yat n tutlayt tddat u twasqdc dg umsawaä am tfransist s imant nns. s unalkam, vas ad tadf tmazivg vur tdblt u tavul d tutlayt n uwank, ad tzlu tafransist tafnsa nns tamattayt (superieur) di lmrruk. s uyad, ituman d is imazivufubiyn tawzn nican tafrankufunit mani ur tagin asnãb n tmazivt. dixn, ur tagin tafransist ad tffv zi lmrruk. 2002 Tawiza. .
