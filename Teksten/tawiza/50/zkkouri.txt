 50, (Juin 2001) Tamazight Surf yi a gma aqbayley Yura t: Idir Zekkouri Cet poème est dédié à la mémoire des martyrs kabyles de la cause amazighe, assassinés par les bes des forces répressive de l'Etat Algerien, a l'occasion de 21eme anniversaire de printemps berbère. Ssurf yi a gma aqbayley, A mmi s n tmagit inu, Nna r ad nghen iodawn, Ioraben ihraymeyn S yism n ddin igan win ku yan. Ssurf yi a gma aqbayley, Acku nsen kullu a ya D yewn unezgum ad nusey. Macan magh ig i txater tasa? Ur zemmaregh ad asfelegh s tudert inu? Ssurf yi a gma aqbayley, Acku meqqar ur ghur i tasa, Pat ddregh d wemdan tuger Winna mi tmezzey drusen Ar agh toadan winna tuger. Ssurf yi a gma aqbayley, Pat key ad yumzvn iman g tmagit negh, Ima nek ghas awal i mi ssnegh D wawal ur d ad itrara ka iddan Daccen a kagh kksen s tadusi. Ssurf yi a gma aqbayley Is tkit idammen nnek ghif tmagit nnegh, Zeglli a ya tsult ar as tikkit Nek anna s kigh idru Acku ur illi watig ghur tudert nnek as tkit. Ssurf yi a gma aqbayley, pat ur ghur i tizemmar Macan ul inu ira ad ibbeozeq S tfeqqah n uorab ahraymey, Ul inu did k a gma aqbayley Idir zekkouri, Meknass, as n 2/5/2001 lexique Ssurf yi: excuse moi asfel: sacrifice tadusi: foce 2002 Tawiza. .
