 50, (Juin 2001) Tamazight Temghi tefsut (d awardi i yiman n Germah Masinisa) Yura t: Abdennajim Lemrabtvi (Gerbas) D acu yedvran i gma ass gi? D acu t yaghen itru s dati? Is d uccen ag tccan ulli? Nigh d attvan irzvagen am ulili? Aghmis ik selleghghas ay ilemzvi Nghan k a Masinisa idvlli, deg wxxam igezzaren, nghan k a memmi. Ur ggwiden tagrawla idlen di tili, Traja tanekra ik a mmis n Tizi. Bgayet a weltma smuqled digi Fekkay d arrac im ur itadjja wehdi, Ceggeo aneghmis i ayt Mzab d lawrasi, Ini asen ayt Wuzzu recmen ifassen s lhenni, Iwaken ad fekken i teslit imensi, Ad temghi tefsut daghen a yessi, Ad zpun imazighen, Leqbayel ad tili. Tamazight a tiqcicin tebgha tilelli, Mlalen wulawen n imazighen a yelli. Abdennajim Lemrabtvi (Gerbas), Tvanja di 04/05/01 2002 Tawiza. .
