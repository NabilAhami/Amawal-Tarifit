 50, (Juin 2001) Tamazight TIGHUYYA Yura t: Kabouri Hassane (Alnif) Righ tamazgha Righ tamazight Righ tammurt Righ tudert Macan ur y udjin. Ssufghen idrimen s tiniri, Ssufghen ten s umuddu inteln, Tqqim tmara ar agh ttawtv, Righ ad ten di raregh Macan ur y udjin. Mani isvntval d imttin? Ffern ten g tmizar ur ssinegh Illin awd g uxdil n islman. Righ ad asen adregh ghur i Macan ur y udjin. Umzvent mraw n takwaten, Tcan dvza n imuren, Zlan amzruy n imazighen. Righ azref ad yili ingwer agh, Macan ur y udjin Tiru d tafsut n timmuzgha, Tafsut n ildjigwen n tmazgha, Ajenjaliy d uzizaw d uwragh, Yili asen uzggûgh g ammas. Macan ur gheyn ad tit sbeddin Kabouri hassane (Alnif) 2002 Tawiza. .
