 50, (Juin 2001) Tamazight AR MANTUR A Y ASIREM? Yura : Idir Zekkuri (Risani) Kullu yiwdan ttrajan c, Is teggudit g wn nnsen, Ar zvarek sbertun atvvas, Ar mantur a y asirem? Ka tegguditv as, ka tedrust as, Mayemmi, nec, asirem inu yedrus? Tugha, ttrajagh c iseggûsen, Ar mantur a y asirem? Ttmuyudegh nec d yitran d wayyur, Tuli d tafuyt, key walu k, Ula rexxu, ur di tusit, Ar mantur a y asirem? Ar mantur a rad di tgulut? Pat yagh i fad nnek atvvas; Ar tteqqlegh ad i d gulun waman nnek, Ar mantur a y asirem? Afad i tekkest fad ddegh i yaghen, Fad n wawal inu, Fad n timmuzgha, Ar mantur a y asirem?
