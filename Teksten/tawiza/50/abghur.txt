 50, (Juin 2001) Tamazight Abghur usar ittesmunen Yura t: Hsayn Oebbud (Arbattv) Azul ad igan assenti n wawal Ila d tatfi g imi amm tamimt. Ar as nettisin asala g d nusa, Arraw n tmazight nbela inigan. Tamazight abghur usar ittesmunen Waxxa am ugin imenbadven tudert , Ran am timdvelt d yili innizan, Gin ger tiseghla d ikarifen. Pa! Tayri nnem tela g wulawen izwan Da tessa tesmulu ar teggar icetvhan Is ur tegi turdut ula azawar. Tamazight abghur usar ittesmunen, Udur n imazighen g tudert nnem. Aburz d wuddur i wass a d usekka. Han win imenza i d usar d yaghul Asey aghanib turu t s usekkil N tifinagh timitar d zin imezwura. Tamazight abghur usar ittesmunen, Iram umussu imal ighudan. Hsayn Oebbud, di 27/3/2001 2002 Tawiza. .
