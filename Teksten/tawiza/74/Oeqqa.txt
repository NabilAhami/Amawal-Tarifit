 Uttvun 74, Sedyûr 2003 (Juin 2003) Tamazight Ur izriy ma yezrin Yaru t: Oeqqa (Amur n Wakuc) Ig sersegh anzgum nnem i mad t ittasin? Ur kwrimnt tiqqad ioetvban ur jjin Sulen dignegh izemlan ur izriy ma yezrin Mennaw n umerwas ad isulen ur t frin Usin d zarnegh uzzal ran ad agh neghin Tkwin agh s tmerdal a nemyitci tigitin Inna pat ur gin xes afgan ur ighwrin As nekkws awal nnes ilemed winnegh yisin Yusi d yan utvad ur as igi yasi d sin Ag irmi yughul yan yadj sa n tinzvadin Ar kemmzen imejjvatv dinna ten ur ittecin Imuhagh, Leqbayl d iIkurdiyen ur ten kwtin Winna g d ur ikkat watvu nnsen ur llin Ass ddegh i LOIRAQ asekka i FILISVTVIN Gan amm tkeskas ammas n tmedwin Mad jun ad iga wazzenbuo lletcin Ur da t id ittekka xes abiba d tburqsin Yuda n waggegen kat icutviyen ad ten hrin Ad d temmeghi tuga neg i yegdvatv ad swin. (Ass n 27\03\2953) 2002 Tawiza. .
