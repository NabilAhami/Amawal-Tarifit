 Uttvun 74, Sedyûr 2003 (Juin 2003) Tamazight Tayrut d ugherdva Yaru t: Icen Saoid (Ayt Ishaq) A tadvfi n ddunit llix n imezwura, Adday skkerex ulli dda ad c munex d iyedvan Hedvun tineggura hedvun ax timezwura. Bnux yan uxam, aseylef ayed as sdirrix Pa taymaret da ttawegh id iyis iqqen dati Yili imal ukan ar ssendux tiyewitin A t'a tverreh a Oica dar tezzvadv tizerbiyin Ad ac beddelex awal wayyadv c ax d wiss sin Awa yuket n terbat iqeran ad xef awen d iwix Tix n ugherdva d uyeru lliy ghur s d idda. Inna s ad nemyawal ghened ad ney imeddukal Tenna s awa rexu s ttawil ad ur zerreb walu Ad ax d iddu baba nu ukan ar netmdacar. Inna s a tayrut n waman nek awer am d yusin Fessusex ur hlix amezvt I qenat zi wedvar. Tenna s illa lbaz nna yawen irrazven ioedjumma Ihuf ghi fs yassi t' pan ayeru igelulla I max mayd rix iwel ad iyebdvan d wacal Awa lapel n teheyutv ur sellan is teya ta Iwa tarewa n lehram imeci d ayed da tegga kul. 2002 Tawiza. .
