 Uttvun 74, Sedyûr 2003 (Juin 2003) Tamazight Titsdis n tilelli Yaru t: Idir zekkouri (Ameknas) Akud iwhel ireggwl, Imazighen sgherrs en da tteqqel en, Iàdawn lan tawuri ur hwwil en, Amezrug ira ad issughul Itsent tghawsiwin idderen isulen Illan i ku yan umazigh g wul. Ad ifegged itsen t itturun, Am iodawn n tudert, Isid kullu ur da t tturun, Ghas aynna ran ayd t tturun. Ttun is tarun medden datas en, S idammen nnsen, Hafden t yasur asen d idammen Acku nitni ayd t isseker n ur id am winna i tturun Aynna urigin winnesen S imcinna ran Af ad a t raren d winnsen. Macan amezrug issen kulla aya Is ibedda ad asen d isnot aya Tabrat n imazighen a D yusey chal aya Isid tarwa n unamur a. Is tbbeozeq titsdis n tilelli Yisil as ku yan mandi g illa Tssikid wenna isul n igna af ad snighisen amezrug a Isin mayd asen inna Af ad ur tettun mayd gan (Idir zekkouri Mohamed, Arfud as n 14/02/1998) 2002 Tawiza. .
