 54, (Octobre 2001) Tamazight Asif n inigiyen Tura t: Aïcha Aït-Hammou Mayed yaghen ayed degh n wasif da ya, Mayed d as innan ka, ig ur da aku itfeseta, Sawelegh as, nnigh as, awa isen terit afella, Inna yi upu, da yagh i widda ittun tala. I wadda ur issin yad mayed iga, ula mas idda, Aret ittawiy uzewu, aret katten t tefikca, Ddagh g ira ka ikes asen, tella dikes taweda, Ggid idda izeriy, ittes isul dikes amm tamena. Nnigh as i wasif pan ayennagh ttinim ur illi, Zerigh medden, ar ttinigen seg lli yat tenutefi, Afad in ass ma yila, ad as d aliyen ar imi, Netta tala uren tebbiy awed pah i yat tanumi. Imettvawen ur llin g xes unna ghur illa wul, Ar ittebbiy, ur yufi mayed as isseker a yghul, Unna mi t inna, yini yak, ddu gen a yaghiyul, Ared ddagh g tenekert, teziret agherun is asul. Tafuyt dad ttaliy ku yass, g igenna yili wayyur, Llan iteran g ygenewan, tellit a tafuyt, g umur, Izeran da sawalen i bu imeziyan, gin as asemun, Awal inew ur igi awed pah i wanna ur itemunn.
