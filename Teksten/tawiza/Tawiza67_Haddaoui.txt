 Uttvun 67, Yemrayûr 2002 ( 2002) Tamazight I yman n Tifsa Yaru t: Muhammed Haddawi ( Oin Llup) Pan anzgum n tmazight illa digi, Ad asix aggwa nsvber yawedv tizi Icayedv wawal ghuri illa yggudi Iommer wul inw tsvart ayed digi Ad as inix i wmazigh awal ighudan Zeghix axam i tmesmunt inu labas. Awa gigh as isem nnay gan bnid as Ata cemm a Tifsa ayeda ytsagan Pan agdjig nnem illa yxalef cigan Idda wzwu d unzvar nnax issergigin, Ddant tillas n swass ddun inidan. Ass a tensidd azzman aoban ighudan. Muhammed Haddwi zeg tmesmunt n TIFSA (Oin Lluh) 2002 Tawiza. .
