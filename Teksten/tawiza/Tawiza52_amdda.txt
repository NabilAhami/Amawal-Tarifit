 52, (Août 2001) Tamazight Timmuzgha d umdda Yura t: Saoid Bajji (Xnifra) Ismun wawal g ires inew Mec ktigh mayd ijran Igudi wawal ur ibbi yires inew Feppim tixet g twungimt inew Taghul tmagit ul inew. Yugh ifesti iya ighuyyan Tlal tsertit g uqcac inew, Yufedv fad zeg ires inew. Temmuti tafult i teghuni inew, Ibedvi izvi g ssaht inew Ika wasif n temmuzgha ul inew, Umdda ayed yix urid amddala. Usigh imrayen yugh wafa g wacal. Is yigh amazigh urid wayadv Righ tamagit atiy agdjuy s imezdagh. Yura t Saoid Bajji, ass: 1 Mayyu 2001. 2002 Tawiza. .
