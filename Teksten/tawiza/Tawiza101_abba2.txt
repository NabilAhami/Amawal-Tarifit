 uïïun 101, Tzayur 2005 ( 2005) Tamazight amcawar n ifggigen f terzi n ughanim Yaru t: Oebba Lhassan ifeld ak yan gh imdyazen yan wawal izri nit aghzan imazighen gh yan wawal lligh as nnan amcawaren ak ifggigen f terzi n ughanim imill rezen ifggigen iqama d ughanim asetta n ghassad ur igi mas ittawi yan afus ibadel t uzemz ur sul ifki tayafut. a sellagh iyyat tenna itayyadv s yan wawal ihennan asetta ifelt uzemz ifelt ugharas. n felt nit n knin dagh ur t ak isala yan igh yasen wayyadv ad ghar ifelt selagh nettan idda udvar inu s ugdez negru tawada nnagh afghen asetta aqbur iga selagh nettan it hit usetta d an ukan iqaman it hit usetta d awer kirin a yafus is nukmat usetta d awer tri tit n yan yenna yas agh zzigh gis iqqazen gh tasa yawi iman nega asetta ur ilin atig ur ak nli tisent ur ak nttaf ida s tnnam ila atig miden xlan ima win berra ahan ur sul ifki tayafut asetta win d mazirt ukan ad tasim a nnan nit dagh imazighen gh wawal izri nit aya zru nt mazirt ay ttawi yan nega win berra ur ligh atig ad wurrigh nik nit ad nfel ad ghar iwada ken ukan ilamman ad dagh nxwu tamazirt nnun nkcem tinnun i rebbi a tamghart nera ad am nini yaw awal a han win berra ur lin atig ur fkin tayafut awin wakal nt mazirt kera s tufit awal amcawaren ak ifggigen f terzi n ughanim imil irez ifggig iqama d ughanim yura t: umdiyaz aaba hassan, idlis nes d amissagh-(tisgar 1) 2002 Tawiza. .
