 Uttvun 62, Sedyûr 2002 (Juin 2002) Tamazight Taghdveft Yaru t: Mensvuru Hemmu (Ayt Ishaq) Asefki i mass Muhemmed Budpan Ur jju nemun, Ula nela asmun, Ur jju nega yan. Da nnezegh zar arewas, Zar anu ihgeban. Aghent ax temlellay. Iberdan jlan. Ussan gan yan. Nega am waddex iran, Ad yamezv azvu, Ad ikerz aman. Am ufric Ittuten g uoeric S ugheruc n ukerruc Afella n uxencuc. Pat isul altu Gher dvaret itoayad Ugenduz ad x itenharen. Widda am ioellucen helan, Tekkat zzi sen rrihet ijjvan N iceoban, Neccar idsen ifran. Tamagit telwedv, Adrar ixla, Attvyen ideykan Imeghid waddak ayezlal War izvuran. Zi zik a tamuret, Tayri nnem d tasvebhant, Ul nnem d axatar, Ighil nnem inerghi, Tadvsva nnem d tafulkit, Awal nnem aregrag, G ul Svtveonan asewen, Skeren azdugh, Am nna ideza, dzan. Ifukk, a tamazirt Ad nezzvu imellan, Ad d nrar ay iddan, S ughewwet S may nela n igerdvan Ad nini anna illan. 2002 Tawiza. .
