 uïïun 100, tamyur 2005 (Août 2005) Tamazight Amkesa Amezzviyan Llan ghurvi ssa n iseggûsen, cfin ay d ad hdvugh ixfawn. Iwrd i semmus deg timirt, la i tssufugh imma zeg tussut iligh la sawalgh d wen uffnt s y itvs. S idvaren ur ilsin, la kkatgh abrid ittawin ghers almu d nekk la ttirirgh. La ttasiygh deg usiy inuw yan ufelluy n ughrvum aqvurvan d imiqq n tiklilt ar tteccagh qqimigh ghif yat tiselli. Deg wagud nnadgh, abda yan unugudv amezzviyan ittuswalefn attvas ur jjin ad iqqim ur d illiy ad as kfgh its ifezvawn zeg ughrvum inuw.Iydi Médor la idveffvur ikruwan igellinen asel ad ten yawedv s kra ixxan maca ad ten irar ghers ulli. Iggudiy manig tsellad ghers iwryen i ubeghghem n kan ighejd amezzviyan la iqqvar i imma ins. Adday tghliy tafuyt d iut y idv la traragh ixfawn ghers adras apa kcemgh ghers tinusnuwi manig tlla la itteganna tsekkift irghvan. (Emile Guillaumin, asughel n Uhmad kessu) 2002 Tawiza. .
