 uïïun 178, sinyur 2962 (Février 2012) Tamazight hkummt n tiknukratvi … yura t: madghis buzakhar (tripoli, libya-tamazgha) udl agh dis lemmi isigh g lqitvar seg napoli in milanu talji (tajji) mi ssnegh yus d g lbal inu ad righ s kra n tawalin f lli yella it-svara g tmura, seg matta mmal nas asuffegh hkummet n tiknukratv lli xsen ssis ad suffghen iwdan s gag n 8 iyyuren g umennugh jar lli it naba s ikkas n uqeddafi (asekkur) d lli yexs it ad yeqqim d iddafeo fellas. tawalin ted pat taherfit xsgh ukan ad mmlagh matta g lxatvr inu, ad qqimen isekkilen s yal tilghiwin d inigan f tamara inu, ahulef inu as afar nnes tira inu. semd ight g djerba ass n 26/12/2011-2961 tama n humet n ssuq, awal mi medda maca nuc as tazwara d tagara ayed netta. hkummt n tiknukratvi … righ ad arigh seg lemmi kemsegh izerfan g imi igigh hruz i tilelli righ ad znigh seg lemmi mmal nas “hkummt n tiknukratvi … “ s imesli, yuli tvatv wammas, wali s izvaf, yugwi tatvsva f ufgan, tawezghi s urrif, yukwi asemdi n tikli, tami mazegh yewwa, yuri s isekkilen seg izri righ ad nkigh t seg lemmi mmal nas “hkummt n tiknukratvi … “ amehras , abuhli d ajetvri izelmazen merrden f amuri slubbdven d wwan tafuri juggen cgughen, alemmi maniya thrzem talji dj … nella ffegh… imir a nuhhel seg yir n tmusna mmal nas “hkummt n tiknukratvi … “ ilaq, ad tawetv mammek nuri… hkummt n tiknukraïi … rib ad ari seg lemmi kemsv izerfan g imi igi hruz i tilelli riv ad zniv seg lemmi mmal nas “hkummt n tiknukraïi… “ s imesli, yuli i wammas ,wali s icaf, yugwi taya f ufgan, tawezvi s urrif, yukwi asemdi n tikli, tami mazeb imir a, yuri s isekkilen seg izri riv ad nki seg lemmi mmal nas “hkummt n tiknukraïi… “ Libya, azmez 17/12/2011-2961 2002 Tawiza. .
