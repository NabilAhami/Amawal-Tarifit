 uïïun 127, ymrayur 2957 ( 2007) Tamazight Ufugh n "Tiggest n wur" Immarni d ghar yiyyar n udellis ijj n udellis n tmazight nneghni d amaynu, ijj n udellis n usefru, day s taqessist d tqessisin, day s awar d tawarin, ijj n udellis n mass said EL FARRAD wanita idjan d adellis amezwaru nnes deg ubrid n tirra nnes icuren s tirra d usneflul. Adellis a asemqar nnes «TIGGEST N WUR» yecti day s 20 n tqessisin tizirarin di 45 n wudmawen n tefray. iffegh d di tarifa graf n berkan, amec tamsizwarut nnes tura t mayssa racida EL MARRAKI, aoban n udellis isulef it mass ZREQTI muhamed . Adellis a itwafsar di qao ighezdisa n arrif, nesitim ad as i wezrawi ad yiri wanita d ijj n usenti yinneghni ad tid dvfaren . (Mayssa rachida EL MARRAKI) 2002 Tawiza. .
