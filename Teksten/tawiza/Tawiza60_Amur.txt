 Amur ameqran Yuru t: Hha Udadess (Rbatv) Lmaghrib loarabi, ass a usin am! Ap Tamazgha, Nna mid yusa umur ameqran. Inna t wacal d igenwan, Azvru d usklu d imedwan. Aorab tiwi t id tezwutt, Ighal is izzva izvuran. Izzewar lequroan, Maca taourrvba ayd ran. Wanna ighalen is cewan, Irna idd ccerif ayd gan. Ad yili, ad inbedv, ad isawel, Ar ittini: Grat i umazigh tikwemmamin, Tabarda d wuzlan i tesweggin. Rarat, iysan d iserdan. Awal, imazighen, adday t zedvaren, Asin wiyyadv: Mani amur? Terzvam loaped ameqqran! Tamazgha, Lmaghrib loarabi, ass a usin am! Ranet ioraben, ranet imenbadven, D yidsen mi nnan d imesnawen. Aneqqer day ayd ten iseghan, Addur mec t lan, ayd zzenzan. Bu tamurt, Acal, ur da kwen ttettan; Amharves ayd issekwent igran. Amezwag ittecan isewan. Imezdaghen, ah Tamazgha, Ur ttun, Imazighen ayd gan. Ioraben, mec akkw llan, Tadersitt, akkw s wurawen. G tuzzvunet ur ttamun; Tardaset day n inebgayen. Hha Udadess, Rbatv g 13/12/2001
