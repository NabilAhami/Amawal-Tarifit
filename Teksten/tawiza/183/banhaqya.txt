 uïïun 183, sayur 2962 (Juillet 2012) Tamazight tiwecca, zi tufut Yura t: viktur hugu, ismzgh it: Hassan Banhakeia Tiwecca, zi tufut, xmini gha imlel barra Ad uyaregh. Aqqa snegh, aqqa trajid ayi snegh. Ad kkegh xaf tagant, ad kkegh x wdrar. War zemmaregh iggwjegh xafm attvas ad qqimegh. Ad uyaregh tittawin mmarsent xf ixarrisen inu, Sembra ad xzaregh ghar barra, sembra ad slegh walu, Necc wapa, aorur ifargh, ifassen qjijwn ayi, Ighurf d wass, am acnaw test aqqa t ghari. War xezaregh ura d uregh n ughelluy i iwttvan, Ura igharruba deg ujemmadv in, ghar Harflur hekkwan, D xmini id gha yawdegh, ad am sarsegh x wmdel Ijj n tsemmut d tazizawt n tuzarin d newwac itcelcel. 2002 Tawiza. .
