 Uttvun 84, Kuzvyur 2004 (Avril 2004) Tamazight Anawlu g Tamazgha S izwal n yakuc amellay d amenpagh Ass n tectrit mnit nettru fellawen ayetmaten nnegh g Rrif mnit mettva yeggez f acal n tamazgha. Ansi yiga asif ad yessiwetv iferfaden nnegh d ighilufen nnegh i knim, amuttel i yegmiren igent ajadir, gara-nagh. Ayetma g tamazgha, amxix isvar i Rrif nnegh n imazighen iderfan. Surf iyid uwwa n Rrif, surf iyid ultma n Rrif sirmagh ad fkagh fus n awwas. Nbedd d nettvutva, ad nedder d ad nemmet imazighen kan……. In yakuc meqqer yanedwl. s ayet n libya iderfan 29-2-2004 2002 Tawiza. .
