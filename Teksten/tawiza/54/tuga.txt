 54, (Octobre 2001) Tamazight Tuga Tura t: Aïcha Aït-Hammou Yagh lazv ayed degh n ufunas, Neddu nawiy as d ka n uferas, D yat tuga afad ay itech as, Ar itesetta ig d ddanet tillas. Da g idejiwen ik agh tiqqar, Innak is terit akesum n utar, Agha k a yema degh amazedar, Tiyeti ared teddut s umeghar. Nnigh as upu pat ur righ ameya, Unna iran akesum iddu s Taghiya, Ikkes dikes tiyeni ar tes itesetta, Ur illi geragh mami nessemera. Nigh as mayed ak gigh ur tessinet, Ar ddagh g texatret ar ttiziret, Is ak righ afella negh tamazedaret, Unna igan ka ifest amm i walut. Tifunasin ddegh agh ittiyen, Mayed asen nnanet tarwiwin, Afa yagh g wul i wunna g llan, Unna ghur ur llin, igen ites nes. Aïcha Aït-Hammou 2002 Tawiza. .
