 54, (Octobre 2001) Tamazight Alaghi i yellis n umazigh Yura t: Brapim Oadili (Zrarda, Tapla, Taza) Llap, a yellis n umazigh, Seghd ghar i ad am inigh, War zemmaregh ad ffregh Tmughli n tittvaw nnem. Tsemxata i inew Zi melmi netc pelcegh. Lligh xsegh ad mtegh. Siwel akid i, lellep ad jjigh A yellis n umazigh, Tucerd ul inew War yeqqim d wenni inew. Tentem t iss ghar ujenna A tadbirt n Taridatt* Seqsegh xaf m Aghbal* d Taniditt* Qao idurar n Fazaz aked Arrif Idj war yezvri manis ttekked, A yellis n umazigh Ad kid m war pnigh Tayri nnem d lebher, Netc ghelyegh dag s, Gguregh degglegh Aked lemwaj nnes Ad inigh yersa Nigh ipaj. A yellis n umazigh Tedjid idammen d adfel Lehmu n yiffan nnem Netta ay dt gh ad yesfesyen Dwel d ghar i a tudart inew Ifukk ad cem afegh, Ad kid m munegh. D cem ay gh ad melcegh, War sar tilid i yidj yadvnin Ma ysar, ad mmtegh. (*) Idurar n Zrarda (Je dédie ce poème à l'âme de ma grand-mère “Yemma Tecfa” décédée cette année et à tous Imazighen) 2002 Tawiza. .
