 Uttvun 75, Sayûr 2003 (Juillet 2003) Tamazight Rix Yaru t: Yyubi Muhemmed (Tamesmunt Imal, Ayt Ishaq) Rix ad nemdacar, rix ad nraoa Hemmu D Ihya awal ad x ttinin Id is tram ad neyat ca ugeraw ighudan Oellemat ax ad nemoawan. Awi tulid tafuyet xef umerdul Imazighen patin aghul siddan Rix ad ssarax, rix ad rezux imazighen Inew mani y nnusan Ufix amazigh yafi iqan d ad nemoawan Kul aya d ighuda Tnna c tmazight kkix ur ar tannayex Aghulex da nessadda Tnna c tmazight ran irumin ad ineghin Ur ssigiren advu Illa wakuc da kwen ikkat asur Teghudam temham awal amazigh (Ayt Ishaq, 28 kuzvyur 2953) 2002 Tawiza. .
