 uïïun 115, ymrayur 2006 ( 2006) Tamazight Tamesghult n ilxiyana Yura t: salah ngab (libya) Si tekker di ulawn nnegh tagda Kan yettazel ghef aghrum yelha baba Aorab yehkem iddin yentvel di zekka D ussem, sussem, sussem yemla Netci tarwa n innabi Ils nnu , ils n rebbi D in iljapiliya yerra di des timura. Nsussem wel nseslec tawalt n la Ara nnegh xedmun aorab, ghessun ilwehda Zvurrun anegh, nedwel igujilen n ilumma D sussem, sussem, sussem yemla Tarwa n Asirem inghun t meckun Taxertilt imlun dvedd i lqanun D am mammu yused s ilhijaz, am ruma. Ilhijab nnsen yedja tamettvut d tayya D argaz tumer n iddin terr t i lghurilla Inghun angh d imlun tuha amrika D sussem, sussem, sussem yemla Yanutlay, wel nufuc iles Imi zeroun dis ighes D izzad nnegh tamesghult n ilxiyana. Amezruy iskerksun, inghun masenissa Neghru kan ghef jemal, sveddam, d usama Imlun kan i ddinasvur yedder di tamezgha D sussem, sussem, sussem yemla Iljennet tella di felstvin d iloiraq Si nemlu libya imlun ibunaq D tadelsa n Bergen tsutva ghasru terna. Tira nnegh – tifinagh – hram s ilfatwa Ilya ad nsefra sis, yanzvun iddula Udayn netcin, maca oemmi tsen musa Nedwel am izyitv n azel Nerwetv zvettvef, nemlu mellel Tagda nngh si negh, neghr t d tagrawla. 2002 Tawiza. .
