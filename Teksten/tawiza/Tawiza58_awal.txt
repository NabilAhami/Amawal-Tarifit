 58, (Février 2002) Tamazight Awal Yura t: Mansvuri Hammu (Ayt Ishaq) I Ssi Muhemmed Mulud d Payur Inna bu wawal: “Kud ntsawal, Yettagh ax fad n wawal, Yuf ifesti Gar awal”. Am nna trid asey, Zi ucelif n umarey. Aneli nec d wul, Dvfar ten ad ur tekkul. “Mer tudert ur terwa D uzvemzv ur ittenyiddu Adj g wanrar arewa Tarejad ad isvudv wadvu” Tawada tughezifet, Tebdeo s yat n tsurifet. Ad ur teggêd iyedvan, Is tzerid d ukabar advan. Pan amexxar, Yuf t umatar, Yaf ten bu wsafar. Wenna inegha ixef, yass. Ku yuwen ittisin ayedda s. Mec tufid. Smun Ad ur bettvu. Ifes nna tezzvidv Hedvut ur t tettu. Sri ad ur krres. Amezv, fek ad ur kmmes Unna c iran iri t. Ayennufar nes ur t teqqis. Unna ur c irin zri t, Hid as, ur dt ttasi, ur siri s. Tawuri, amarey d tusna Krattzi ur iteyaqqal yan, Mec ira tudert ighudan, D wayelal nes g iyenna. Tanebbadvt ad taley, Aggwa ad issisey, D waneli nes ad ipenna. Mun d ubrid ittveonan, Ad ur c wweten isennan. Ddu g usidd Ur ixxi xas, seres May tusid, Ur zzvioen fad, Waman irewiyen. Ula kessen lazv, Ifriruyen. Tuttilet iyedjuyen, Tumaret iggafeyen D tanemmirt ciyan, I ku yan. Ayt Ishaq, 27/8/99. 2002 Tawiza. .
