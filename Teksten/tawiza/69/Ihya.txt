 Uttvun 69, Yenyûr 2003 (Janvier 2003) Tamazight Ictab i rebbi Yaru t: Ihya Muhammed (Ayt Ishaq) Ictab i rebbi ad annayax anna ijran, Annayax aytma ar ten tettan islman Lliy ran ad bbin aman Ad awdven nna ihlan, Zvrin axub d isnnan. Ictab i rebbi ad annayax Yuk zi timizar n oribban Ddan d ar ssaran, Nnan: Nra ca n icirran nna iqran, Ad ddun gher ansa n ilghman, Ad ccin swin ilin igherman. Ictab i rebbi ad olux aynna yan Ar smuttur iy smuter cigan Yin am tin ssitlan, Ur nssin mani zi kkan. Annayax aytma dat n lberlman S lazv d fad llan qqiman Mani ken a widx as n innan Away d 18000 nican Tddum ar d iken sulan Uma dghi a ayt ihrbillan Tyam asen omed iydi fghen t yiman. A ayt izrfan n umaddan, Krat tyim inigan Tgrim ighuyyan S yidvan d wussan Ad rarin aytma aynna llan. 2002 Tawiza. .
