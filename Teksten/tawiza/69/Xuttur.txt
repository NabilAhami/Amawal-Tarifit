 Uttvun 69, Yenyûr 2003 (Janvier 2003) Tamazight Tinit n umuni Yura t: Saoid Xuttur (Pulandda) Ma d izref x anegh, ad ninit(1) amuni, Neccin nttarvu war d nejji aseymi. I mara nnigh awem ksit ayi d izvri, Nigh gget ayi d kenniw s yijj n yimi Neccin nttemseksi jar anegh x urehmi Maca oad war nuri x iseqsiyen n unami. D wakud ijja negh yarenni deg wmari, Urawen nnegh rettxen war nessin mayenzi. Tuzzeyzewt n Tifeswin mechar war teghmi D arrimet inu yemmûn s rxezrat war teksi. D kenniw yesran min yedjan war yedji. Yesmun anegh bettvu yejja anegh d amsebdvi (2001) 1-Tinitin n temghart 2002 Tawiza. .
