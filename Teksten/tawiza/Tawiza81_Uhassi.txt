 Uttvun 81, Yenyûr 2004 (Janvier 2004) Tamazight Tuccka tafuyet Yatu t: Oebdellap Uwahssi (Aghbala n ayet sseghman) Tuccka tafuyet pac tillas g wacal meghar cem usigh a talfettact ur ssuddigh ca ikkad lehal en abrid imray adar da ytnhicci afud icna. Pac tillas g wacal tafuyet tuccka ghifi tagit a tazaret ur am qqazegh yad ansa gigh cem i lajer tiydi memmi d amya. Pac tillas g wacal tafuyet awra ssuger tannayed ayma bu yeghrem g tmaziret nnes magga amzdagh n imddulla arraw nnes da tetter lehal iqima digi a yema (Ass n 13/12/2003) 2002 Tawiza. .
