 Uttvun 64, Tamyûr 2002 (Août 2002) Tamazight Tactut wis kradv n wengha n Lunis: Ighid n tlelli Yaru t: Oadili mmis n Izerruden Ighid n tlelli, ttugha t’ di tmurt nnes yethewwas Zi Tawrirt n Musa, gher Tizi Wezzu. Itveffver (idveffver) peddu, irdzu x yemma s. Yekkes mawwus, ijeggu deg wjenna. Ur as nefpim awal, ur ya nerri x tabrat. Yuhel deg negh yetlagha. Aodaw yebda yetrjiji, ur yezmir ad yesvber. Yqqsa s uzellif, yeggêd x llif. Ighid n tlell di tmurt nnes yeggur di laman, Ioedawen n tudart wejden as am yitvan, Anegmar n tfaft (tfawt), deg wbrid imedya s taxcebt. Neghin t' wuccanen... Zeg izumal, negh zeg ayt umar, Qao d iodawen n tlelli. Uzzelen idammen... Ul d tamzwart, ul d tanggart, Ad yessw umazigh tamurt n tlelli. Tamazgha tamqqrant... Ad c nemtvel a Lunas, Ad nini mayen tennit: "Widen ggulen agh ssrun, d nitni ad irun" "Ulac ssmah ulac" 2002 Tawiza. .
