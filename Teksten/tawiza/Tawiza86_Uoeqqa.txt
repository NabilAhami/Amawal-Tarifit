 Uttvun 86, Sedyur 2004 (Juin 2004) Tamazight Ur batvegh Yaru t: Oeqqa (Amur n yakuc) Ur batvegh a tasa mek gwerrehen idammen Ixebaren n tayri gan zund win idmamen Mad agh yiwin a tasa s ujeddig n imlalen Ad t nestara seg Oileqemt ar nnig Bumalen. Ur ad k batvegh a yimi ig as zwan imettvawen Ig isawl uzwu g urennar, ig walu imettvaren. G unebdu, da ttekka tawerghi tanjayt n uzelag Ibedd unebedd ar d iwehel, iqqim d waddag Ur ad k batvegh a yafus g d mmeghint tilfagh Ig d usigh agelzim, gweregh amud n tfinagh Ad d immeghi wawal inw g udem i wmelal Ass nna g ittver udelal zund tawrawra g wakal. Ur ad k batvegh a yul inu mek tergagt Ddag tannayt g tefsut asmun inu s lonayt Ddag tessudda tafuyt i wargalen s tifawt Negh tsellat i tmilla tekwcem s uketef tjawt. Ur as batvegh i tghufi nnek a DADS ig i tagh Qad seghlegh id oari, kkegh tizi g d lmalagh A bu wudem immummin bu tferkit tazeggwaght Pan unna kwn ur irin pan amuttel nnek pat yaghet. 2002 Tawiza. .
