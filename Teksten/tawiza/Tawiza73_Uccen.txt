 Uttvun 73, Semyûr 2003 (Mai 2003) Tamazight Uccen d yinsi Yaru t: Dilai Essalih (Ayt Ishaq) Ar iteddu inesi dat n wuccen. iy yannay tiqeddidin dat as. Iberrem gher wuccen, inna yas: «Weti s ubariq». -Max? Irura s wuccen -Nnix ac weti. -Cek oemmi ucen, ar teddux dat ac. Iwett s ubariq, inna s: -Oayed gher daret, ur da teheccamed. Iddu wuccen dat n yinsi. Yannay tiqeddidin. Iberrem gher inesi, inna yas: -Matta tiqeddidin a? Tin umuttel, ur da teffghent xas g warraw n warraw ic. -A pent teccex, iney as ad ffghent disen. Itec tamezwarut, izayed tis snat, tettviqes tseqquret tmezvet. -Tesehlilled ghifi dic tin bbas n bbac. -Pat da tezeyyar ghifi. -Yeret g waman, ad tfsus. Iyera t wuccen g waman, tezayed tezeyyer ghfes. -Pat n tezeyed azveyyer, inna s wuccen. -S ttawil ad t nannayed zzviyaren unna t ibnan. A d c kkaten ared yufu. Irur as yinesi. 2002 Tawiza. .
