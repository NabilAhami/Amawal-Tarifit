 uïïun 117, ynyur 2007 (Janvier 2007) Tamazight taxertilt n amedyaz Yura t: Salah Ngab (libya) Di terjit n amedyaz isligh Taslit nnu tedwel d tadjalt Imettvawen di tameghra nnu izrigh Itcurun awal nnu di tabuqqalt. Wel ucigh netci iddregh imligh Ghersun tadbirt nnu tamellalt Tinnegt s tasusmi urigh Tadsva nnu islunt t tawalt. Sid netci meccek icfigh Uraregh dides tamarit Tsisaw urighes d ighrigh Nurar Asli d Taslit. Warra n tayri urigh Agemmay ul nnu yuri t Inegh in udmis ifkigh D temgher assa tabucilt. Temlu meghregh, cek ittgh Iddjigh tayri nnu tgharit Di ammas n abrid utvigh D ukzegh zug n Tajedjit. 2002 Tawiza. .
