 uïïun 103, ymrayur 2005 ( 2005) Tamazight Tamyurt n ussgurem tellul numidya akcar d uffus n babas d imass lli igulen ur ten glayen gar idagen f tamagitenssen. mayad nit anzran ligh urun arraw izwaren tafruxt ssaghnass ‘’numidya‘’ lli gan gh issmawen n tgeldit n imazighen. smdn issm ad yat gh temyurin n imazighen as tigan d‘’tamyurt n ussgurm‘’ amzwaru izwaren itasant. araqran aydarssen n warraw inaragen; ar sskaren tafuglla mzin. rikad nit aysker babass n numidya; teg yat gh timyurin negh lli ssdiqan imazighen asset sskaren urd ahlli tadran. ayuz ibabass n numidya akcar f tawuriy ad nss. tudert i numidya tudert i tmazight (Amnay Samir) 2002 Tawiza. .
