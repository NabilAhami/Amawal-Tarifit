 uïïun 106, sinyur 2006 (Février 2006) Tamazight Yelli maren Yura t: Salah Ngab (libya) Yelli maren tbark ap ghfes Awal yezvam yemmal illes Wil tesenc ad teskerkes D may immaleghs tessal Tezerzer temmeml nnes Tessen rabi tuzvum tzval. Taziri di wudem nnes dunit ad tedvwa ilya tedves Izates m dis taourt teqqes Tessen ad tari sis zvetta Ad tezvregh di island nnes Ad tegg ilhenni ad terwetv tlaba. Yelli maren macap ghfes Tamezwart illan deffres D rummu am netta iyghes yelllis Di lbal nnu dima tella Test s ass nnu tekkes Si temlyed a baba. Tugurya nnes af idles Tessen ad twaza temdukl nnes Adbir ghrdses yetves Teklwt nnes azul tmmal A yakuc tanmirt flles Yellis n adrar amellal. 2002 Tawiza. .
