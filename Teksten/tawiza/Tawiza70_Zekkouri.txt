 Uttvun 70, Sinyûr 2003 (Février 2003) Tamazight Attvan n usoarreb "La maladie d'arabisation" Yaru t: Idir Zekkuri (Meknass) Ssaragh timizar n unamur inew, Sllagh i ywaliwn, Medden dik sen ya wattvan, Ur t zvrin, ur as d iwiyn acal. Yasvur asen d idammen, Ur ssin mandi g tusin. Ula mandi ad sen d ikka. Macan iman asen d d uzwu n ccerq, Timitar n wattvan ddegh. Bnadem lga sin Amazigh ig aorab. Mec d ysawal tinit amazigh, Macan iwaliwn nnes, D imcinna s itxemmam Am waorab. Ayddegh ayd igan attvan n uorreb A yayet loaqel d ayt war loaqul Idiwan n wattvan ddegh Ceqqan! ghlan! Acku iseggasen aya dig nnegh illa Macan wunna iran pat ad ijjey Acku nssen amentil nnes. Pat aorreb awsngim ayt igan Addag did s nebdvu. Ad npenna g wattvan ddegh. (Muhmed Zekkouri Idir, ass n/05/11/2000 G Nnadvur) Amawal Itxemmam: pense - Amentil: la cause - wsngim: idéologique 2002 Tawiza. .
