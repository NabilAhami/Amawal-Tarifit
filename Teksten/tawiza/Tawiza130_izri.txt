 uïïun 130, sinyur 2958 (Février 2008) Tamazight min tirigh? Yura t: said el farrad ttirigh d abupari! xmi ttiri tighit tennay x ucarcur tghir as tbed x ubeddur tarzzu yas d i tubuperya ca n dewra zeg weddar...!!? mani tirigh nec d mighis nettat teddar tfuct s busiyar, tirigh d aqubbani? xmi tighri tebhid i wraghi i d as di tarra wedrar!? Itghir as tamwarit zeg wemghar...!? mani tirigh nec ghrigh nettat tfetter d timenna i ydepcar, tirigh d atarras? xmi tafegh i yixf inu deg ixf n wenedni umas tirawin nsen tizi ghars ij n yemmas tfuct manis ghad tek tnedh iten gha rmas: nec d atarras ...! nec d atarras...! nec d atarras...! (said el farrad) 2002 Tawiza. .
