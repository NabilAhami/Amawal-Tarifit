 uïïun 104, mggyur 2005 (Décembre 2005) Tamazight Tlalit n tmesmunt n tudert ass n 16 /09/ 2005 g tmazirt n Msmrir g warzazat skrin imghnasn n umussu adlsan amazigh agraw marad skrn tamsmunt n tudrt. G tizwri isawl uslway n ljamoa n msmrir ghf anzgum na da tasint tmsmunin mard ttdu tmazirt ngh s lkddam tighrdin ns. Iswl umghnas aqdim n ummusu amazigh g tsdawit n ugadir burda lhussayn ghf tmsmunt n tudrt inna ms tnna ad tc atig i wfgan g tmazirt n msmrir. Ddaras skrn lbiru n tmsmunt iddu imki aslway burda lhoussayn (president), mnzars (vice) irih hassan, amaru ayt omar hassan, manzars nuri muhmmed, buighridn lhyani brapim, manzars hassan buwid, unaghadni muxtari lhussayn trhi hassan qac hassan said bwuidi kaml abd lmujud. (burda lhoussayn) 2002 Tawiza. .
