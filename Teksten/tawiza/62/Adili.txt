 Uttvun 62, Sedyûr 2002 (Juin 2002) Tamazight Ayt Warayn, Imezdagh n twattut (Ayt warayn, les citoyens de l’oubli) Yaru t: Oadili, mmis n Izerruden (Tapla, Taza) Awarayn yetcehh (ixeyyeq), Lhal ghar s yeqbeh, S nnej i Fazaz, s wadda irrif, Zeg upermemmu al Gersif, Din (dinni) yezdegh aked negh lhif, Di tazekka, tamazight manis tekka? Di Tapla, ssvber isala, Ayt lferh, ipejr iten lferh, Izerruden, yugg ad yekkes lghben, Deg ughbal, ur awen nelli di lbal! Tabhirt d ukawan, mani llan iberdan? Tazarin d buyblan, lhegra tella tban! Aperememmu terrim t d Ribatv Lxir Maca teqtvwem xaf s lxir. Tanidilt, din tetwaqper taqbilt, Ayt Oeli, tanukri ur telli! Tighunam d yefran, din bercenen wussan Tettum anegh di temurt a Walu tefaft, walu aman Iberdan...? iberdan...? Ku aseggûas, ttinim nnubt a, Awarayen yehzen, yenghi t lmexzen, Iweddver jar temurt n irumiyen d sserbis, Tettum anegh di temurt a, Tessenm anegh ghas di sserbis, Teyrim angh x idis, Tessemeghrem aoeddis. (i yiman n ayt warayn n teswa ssvhra d yinn yetwatvfen gher lbulizaryu, i tamettvudvt tawaraynct) 2002 Tawiza. .
