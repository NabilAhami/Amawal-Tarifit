 uïïun 143, krayur 2959 (Mars 2009) Tamazight ankraf amazigh Yaru t: lhou ichou de taghya ilmchan yuli d yitij n tlilli ad t ifru umazigh s idamnis dasin ukan agari di wudm n waodaw ni rzat ifsti nun ssighat afa fad awrigin aghad ula aggu attid itllf wassif. yaghli d waorab s tmazgha yukr akal isgl awal immut ufgan g laman yannay marur d aman ad yayl utbir bu twngimt fad ad smdurrun aglif sxsyat tjjajt i tifawt nkr a mmis umazigh. kullangh ngawk inagan nzrawk tarfufnt nni gilla warrac amazigh nggul ur ttughul titar mqqar nghan lmatub sekku muhmmad d ankraf slimane uoli d wiyyad marra ur tnawk ittudu umzruy. 2002 Tawiza. .
