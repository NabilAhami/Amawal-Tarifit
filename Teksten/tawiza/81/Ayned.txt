 Uttvun 81, Yenyûr 2004 (Janvier 2004) Tamazight Taksista “Taksista” d ict n Tamezguntura t u yirar it Mustafa Ayned di Pulanda. Toawad tudart n ayt bab n Taxi di tmurt nnegh. Tudart yenzedmen jar ibriden iqeroen d rmexzen yeffuden arrecwet. Mhand yewca cwayt zeg ukud nnes huma ad ioawed i yiwdan min xas itekken d min xas ikkin. Oad war ikemmer thajit nnes itbedda d xas bu sulusyun (Kh. Mourigh) itettar zi Mhand ad t’ yecsi ghar ujenna mani ixs ad yebna ict n teyzart temghar ad ayes yeddar haca netta, yeggêj x yiwdan i t’ icarpen. Oad itemqetmar Mhand aked Bu Sulusyun uca ad xaf sen d ibed abarlamani (A.Sellami) yurezzu wi t’ ghad ioawnen huma ad ghar s tedwer temghart nnes i d as yuyaren. Ad ssiwren, ad mzawaren, ad swen, ad cedvhen maca afeqqus n Mhend yeqqim d wenni d nnetta, afeqqus n Bu Sulusyun yeqqim d wenni d netta, afeqqus n ubarlamani ad yeqqim d wenni d nnetta. Ad cren, ad ssensen temruccayen war ten ibettvi mghar refjar mani i d ifekkar Mhend mmis i yecca rebhar. Mmis i tugah yenna ad xas yecsi Taxi hadar ad yeoqer x cwayt n wussan n repna. Script/regie décor en music: M.Ayned, Rol: A.Sellami, Kh.Mourigh en M.Ayned, Technician :K. Haddouch. Taxista a tirar twara tamezwarut di Fistival n Nokour di Gouda/NL di 20 december 2003.
