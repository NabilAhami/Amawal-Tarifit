 Uttvun 81, Yenyûr 2004 (Janvier 2004) Tamazight Ayt Ishaq Yaru t: Ihya Muhememd (Ayt Ishaq) A tamazirt n ayt Idla, a tamazirt n imeghnasen yammun g imenghi n Lpri d imenghi n Tzezawt. Mmuten iryazen, mutent taytcin d ioerrimen lliy sersen tirsal d ihemmar, ssilin tixamin, ikkes unzgum yrin midden tililiwin. Tya tlelli afella n Zzawit tigdjjuyin, nesan iwetman izennar, yrint twetmin tibizarin lliy zveoen arumi yinam a zzawit tiwenziwin. Uma dghi tya ghifn x tadduyt tioeqqidin rezvan idval, bbin ifassen nya tikemmusin. May d ad x ikkusan mami nya wins d ax ttasin niwdv acal tengha x teghufi nehdva iwaliwn tengha x tamara ntetter ad aghuln irumin ur yad telaqd a zzawit. Tyid am ughyul nna iffidin. 2002 Tawiza. .
