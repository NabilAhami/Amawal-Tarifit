 Uttvun 62, Sedyûr 2002 (Juin 2002) Tamazight Izem n tanidilt Yura t: Oadili mmi s n izerruden (Tapla) Brra imi yetzemmummeg, Jaj ul iteqqes. Uhlegh... Xsegh ad ttvuqsegh, Ad sghuyyegh deg wjenna, Am izem n tanidilt. Zzpir inew d ameqran, Ad as teslem zi ku amcan, Isul yejeped. Aodaw zza ges iterjiji, Ma tettum itvlli? Mayen yigh di Zrarda, Tapla d Bupdli, Seqesat x buyblan! Ma yellukk urumi din di ca n wmcan? Tagwdi... Ghar i ur telli. Zegg uccanen negh zegg ulli. D ggwarayen netc, Netc d mazigh, Uled d arumi, Uled zi lyaman, Asusem ur ya yelli, Xsegh tmazight d telelli. 2002 Tawiza. .
