 Uttvun 67, Yemrayûr 2002 ( 2002) Tamazight Cek d abrid i xef ggûregh Yura t: Muhammed Amtales (Danmark) Cek d abrid i xef ggûregh a win yettamaren, immri war yaddji d cek nec war ttmuttuyegh. Tikri ssvurufegh d tikri n bu mardvas yerazzun x tcuni ghad yirin d asegged i we brid yunnuvden. Cek d anpezzi, cek d agenfi i weydud umi nghin tizemmar. Yessitim ad trid d aghemmuy n wetnu ghar yydewren d asekru war yytarezzin. Ad xafek gharen rrmazzez i tidett n umezruy ntan sadu car. Zzay s farnen jjajer s medwast war d ay s tejji rputtic yessampafan rghaci. A win dipa yessrughen tifawin x tin, ad win dipa war i ttwirin. Cek d abri i xef gguregh, a wen yettamaren,Temzvart war tezdighed tedwer marra di farjujen mani ma yella ca n rawqi din ig ittkebba fajjrid yessapracen. Asfer nnegh ixessv ad naxbec icabbrawen huma war tghimi tergast nnegh d rewdeh i yibriden. Neccin d tarwa n tmurt d imeghnas, da yettoanen amerdvas. s wemjar d weffdvis war yeddji di warruden i tnuffuren sadu wa xrijj. maca rxtu war yaqqim bu raji, war yeqqim bu dudart ig rhuttic yeccin tiseqqar. ta d tammurt nnegh day s i ghad nessigh fungu ad nezzgher ig wa kud di ghar nessmun ruob x tussna nnegh yejja weddrib d amessuci. neccin rebda nedja war yeddji d sddjah war yettmut-yen, neccin d addji s twengint i ttxarrasen. mayemmi tugim ad tenpazzem xu sidud yeqqarsen u yak yeqqars yeqqars umin ttrajam war s taddjifem? cek d abrid i xef gguregh a wen yettmaren. muhammed Amtvals zi Danmark, ass n 25-09-2000 2002 Tawiza. .
