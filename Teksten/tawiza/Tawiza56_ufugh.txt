 56, (Décembre 2001) Tamazight Ufugh n tesfift tamezwarut n aygaw Rrayes Muhemmed Ben Yehya Utaznaxt. Issufegh d unazvur “Lmuxtvar Oellali” yat n tesfift igan d tamezwarut nnes. Atig n tewiri ddegh illa g usiwel nnes ghef tamagit tamazight, teg yat tumut (contribution) g tanekra n wefrak (conscience) ammas n taperet (ville) n Warzazat, d tikkas n tillas ghef ugwug amazigh i kigan n imezdaghen n Warzazat itteyaledayen s iberdan ur igin azeghur nsen. G tesfift ddegh, isawel unaruzv “Oellali” ghef tamagit g tadeyazet mi isselegh: Advavdv, g ittini: Advadv, sin, kradv Lulan kullu gh yan uzemz Mnaggaren idvudvan, myafan Gh ufusi, issefled yan i yan. Tayri walabudd ad say ik yan afusi. Advadv, sin, kradv Mnaggaren ifassen, msiwilen Gh ubarazi, seduyen tiqbilin. Advar walabudd aymun d idvudvani. Advadv, sin, kradv Zund ayyur d izvenzar n tafuyt ffghen i dduniti Gh yudv wala azal Tifut walabudd i ddwam gh ugharasi. Advadv, sin, kradv Tanemmirt i tamunt, ad temyar gh ufusi Issefled yan i yan, ikerz lxiri Tagwnaret n tagwmat thubba gertuni Afus gh ufusi immattin wawali Ad ddren izvuran kullu gh yan ufusi Uten s izvuran zerin s igenwani. Advadv, sin, kradv Nekkattin kullu gh yan uzemz Ur nefpim yati s tu tibratin Azemz ad gh lligh Advadv, sin, kradv Nederen izveran gh udrar , Tabya Rahend asif isegher yan gh yani Nederen izveran gh adrar n tabunt Rahend asif isegher yan gh yani Ttifaq walabudd aymun d izvurani. Advadv, sin, kradv Yugem tagut aman n tagwmat Iggig iwet ad awen … aman Anzvar walabudd isman aman gh wasif Advadv, sin, kradv Tacelhit, tmazight, tarifit Lulant kullu gh yan ufus. Iseman tadeyazet: Lhusin DMAMI 2002 Tawiza. .
