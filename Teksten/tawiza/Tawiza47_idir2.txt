Mid zilen ghur nnegh imendiden Ar mantur arad nsseker ighfawn Awd iwaliwn ddan dagh ttackan. Awal nnegh g ugherbaz ur t naru Mk idd win nsen nssen at t nuru Awd ils ur yad da issefru Ghas awal nnsen is issefru! Mc njmeo ar ntssa ard n ssufu Nkmla s ard da nstsu Ard nenker ku yan iftu. Iodawn n tugdut pat oddan Kkan gragh am yirkan ig agh bdan Tfurn laman nnegh igagh zlan, Ssunagh kullu lmhayen.
