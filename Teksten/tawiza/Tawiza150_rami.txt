 uïïun 150, mrayur 2959 (Octobre 2009) Tamazight tast: Ger qwen en d terzmed tent tekkatt inn g igherm llig lulex, yat tmettudvt d illis, kkin sttin ar zzigizent ig ttasent, mayann iga dar sent tamyurt.. ! g yan unebdu, tuzzvumt n yan yidv inegdven g ifessi d ufulki, tenker d dagh tmettudvt d illis (meklli meyarent), ar zzigizent meqqar ttvasent, tikklit ad g tizgi nnesent tdel tbasvurt. tsawel d tmghart tenna i yillis: -«ad kkem yilih a tamaghuzt ihercen, kemmin ad isennstemen afulki inu, tskut tudert nnem s ibladv n tinu! ahh meradd is ufix ad kem neghex!» terar as d tqqcict tenna as: -«a gar tamettudvt isghunfan, a tawssart iqqebern ur issenn amer agayyu nnes, a tad lli y ikkan ger ayyi d ixef inu iderfen, a tad iran ad teg tudert inu taghlaghalt i tudert nnes ittcan ineparmadven , mera ka tafudvet!» akud ann ighwra ufullus, duynt d s yat teklit g idves, meqqar ar ukan sul ttidunt g turtit. tsawel d temghart s kigan d ufulki: -«izd kemmin ayad a tatbirt inu?!» terar as tarrawt nnes s ils immimen: -«yah, illi m ayad a taberzzuyt n tasa inu!» (Yaru t s taorabt: jebran xalil jebran, zeg tawckint nnes «ikabaren d uhyudv», asughel s tmazight: belaid rami azwaw, 15-06-2006/2956) 2002 Tawiza. .
