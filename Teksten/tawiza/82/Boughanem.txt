 Uttvun 82, Sinyûr 2004 (Février 2004) Tamazight Neccin d isudjass Yura t: Bughanem Xalid (Fnideq, Tittvawin) Tudart deg ul inek txeyyeq. Ass a yarrezv. Kkar.. bedd.. arrimet n wussan negh, d asvemmidv x anegh yessuhrut. "Ayt bab n iwajen" s igiman, ar gharnegh zvwin d. Ulin.. pwin d.. nekkin d imetlao gi tgemmi nnegh. "ayt bab n iwajen", ccin.. swin.. uran.. nekkin deg afa n uwemradan negh, neffar.. nkemmedv ar tirvi n wayawya. Tafukt tgeod d, tesseqsa x anegh, tufa negh nessudjes. Nettat' tezvra negh. tru x anegh amettva, neccin urid nezvr it. neccin urid nzvarv. maolik irvi nzvarv, Ad taf u(r) neqqim d isudjas gi tadjest ntexnunnus midden marra x asen tfuyt. (Ass n 15-11- 03) 2002 Tawiza. .
