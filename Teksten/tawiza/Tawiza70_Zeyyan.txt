 Uttvun 70, Sinyûr 2003 (Février 2003) Tamazight Tamazirt inu Yaru t: Mestafa Ziyyan I tmazirt inu, LATLAS bu ticcaw n loezz zeg izvuran n wul. Mas da nttiri tamazirt inx mas? Mas da t swalaf ur id am ti iy ighran? A LATLAS iyyan llun idammen is rwan Rwan zeg ighsan n lejdud inx immenghan. Kkan yits sya yan imazighen iharran, Arun s ummezruy insen addur mas kkan? S win isummal kkad ayt lyaman, S wi ityubbin, s wi issuddan S Misver, s aqbbad ayt ifaroan, Ay asif n iyel manit amazigh s isamman ? Sarfen llaghriq a Hirudut yac llan? Izvra ten lfiniq a macta d ayt rruman Ikka ten unadlus id imazighen as fnan Ddun d loarab ar ittidjimen “llaxwan” Ad ix winsen ad ix am iddis ax sghan Hacawn a Lahrar at im i dduyt isamghan. Max tannajlam ay imazighen iw amdan? Milan tigumma ya xef tuggam? milan Lebhur mi da temdaddam d isalman Milan yar rray ax isuggur? milan Amuttel ax ituggan nniyyax? milan Ifassen ax iyyan iwarya d ax semraran.
