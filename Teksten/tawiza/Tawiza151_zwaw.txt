 uïïun 151, ymrayur 2959 ( 2009) Tamazight tast: titt Yaru t: JEBRAN XALIL JEBRAN, isoarb it: Belaid RAMI AZWAW (Anfa) Tenna titt yan wass i tismmunin nnes timeslay ttsyafanin: -«Dufex ukan g tgherdvin n isaffen ad yan udrar idel umedlu, mmanik ad izvil.. ! » Isella umzzugh yan imikk i wawal nnes, isawel d inna yas: -«Manza adrar an lli tdvufet, nekkin ur ar as tesllax.. ?! » Isawel d ilemma ufus: -«G mad seri izvlin, mmaghex ka ad t syafax, nnegh as ggerx min tayafut, ur illi dinna meqqar d adrar!» Tenna yas tenzert: -«Nekkin ggummix ad akwzex manik s illa udrar, nekkin ur ar as ttekdvux, adrar ad ur illi g tilawt!» Tegrawl nit titt s tesga yadvnin, ar tdvessa g tfekka nnes; timeslay ttesyafanin an yadven nettenti, munent, sekernt yan n ugraw, mediwnnint gis xef mad yudjan, intu titt ad tegnna man ibadvan ad, akud llig bapra seghlent, zedgent ssaffent taghawsa ad, meccackan d s umata «wis ur telli turda mas d titt tengara d unelli nnes..!» (jebran xalil jebran zeg tawckint nnes: «ikabaren d uhyudv», asughel c tmazight: belaid rami azwaw, casa-anfa : ass n : 14-06-2956 /2006) 2002 Tawiza. .
