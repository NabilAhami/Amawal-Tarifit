 Uttvun 65, Tzayûr 2002 ( 2002) Tamazight Adlis nghed Leqran n Iberghwaten (The book or the Coran of the Berghwata) Issaghul t zeg Tangliziyt gher Tamazight: Mbark Aderbal Adlis nsen, lli d issafegh Salih Ben Tarif i ymazighen, dik s tammraw n tassvurt amata. Dik sent usin d ismawen n imazan n Yakuc zeg wakud n Adam.Tassvurt tamezwarut das ttinin Yub, d tamggarut das ttinin Yuhenna (Jonah). Znifen (parmi) tammraw n tassvuratin ad tella tassvurt n Firoun, tin Qarun, tin Paman, tin Pajuj u Majuj, tin Tedjjal, tin uzger n wurgh, tin Parut d Marut, tin Cawul, tin n nimrudv. Illint digh itsent aghent g timucup a: tassvurt n ufullus, tin n tsekkurt (perdrix), tin n tmurghi, tin n ulghem, tin n ifigher itteddun ghif tam n idvaren. Adlis ad ismatter awd tassvurt n sa n talrewin n umadval (seven marvels of the world). Imazighen n Berghwataiga ghur sen udlis ad ameqran ar zvar s seksiwen (smuqqulen) am ughbalu (azurz) n iwaliwen ddernin (sagesse). (Issaghul t zeg Tangliziyt gher Tamazight: Mbark Aderbal zeg “The Berbers in Arabic Literature” n H.T Norris, tifert 103. Norris, H.T (1982) The Berbers In Arabic Literature, London: Longman) 2002 Tawiza. .
