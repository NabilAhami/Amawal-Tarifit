 uïïun 105, ynyur 2006 (Janvier 2006) Tamazight Imazighen n sserbis Yaru t : Lhusayn Burda ig da nssugur may ilan g tmazirt ngh n imazighen n ssrbis, ar ntvfa mas ggudin. Nkin g twriqt dgh rad isswiligh ghf widagh izryen inghmisn n tmazight g tlfza zund asnat ayt tgudn adin ghir "azul". Asdgh ar tinin ur hcimn ula robn masd imazighn aydgan, ixssva ad isin id mqar da tinin dgh "azul" isdatn ur ntamn; masn nsn isamun g wida ira ingh tmazight ur da asn itfgh lmghrib lorabi imaow amid daxfs tamznka, maca txtn tassa nu dasn bbin aghrum, ucn g ddl nnant imzura yut tklt ayd istssa idi ign yagh aman, mas ur yucki iwdan mass g an n ssrbis da zruy inghmisn n tmazight tga diksn taorabt amnasf akw ur s is sn tifinagh ulini mqar ntu amzruy ur ad itvtu, mrid iss ira i irkam ad tddu tmazight s wudm uqad hrdn iclhiyen n ssrbis skcmn mdn issawal s tamazigh ghfis itcan amurad ghifss yanugn isk ghifss isnfluln ar issawal s tmazight n dzaer da cn tin lmghrib uli ni nsn isrdagh, zubbin ighf g uman ar dagh ifgh rruh, in wawl ad t inigh i imazighn mitdu tmasayt g idamn ad qran, ar tinign sicku ighzif ubrid tillawt ad t inin ad t inin. 2002 Tawiza. .
