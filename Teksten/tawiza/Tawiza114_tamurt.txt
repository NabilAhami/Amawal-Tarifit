 uïïun 114, mrayur 2006 (octobre 2006) Tamazight A Tamurt Yura t: uhmad kessu Amsudn imniren... Zennubya! Matta uya! amun d urumiy ma s tumen d Mmuttin igruren... teccid Ikel niwrasen nnem a Dipya Mayd iogebn...Tin-igruwan ur tudir... Gan igherman d imedvghasen ... Ayucen d usin ilghuman... Ghzan timedvlin tigezzay ghif wudem Aqrvurvh ameghlal i winna ikettin... ghzan waggayen imudval nnem... Moligh ccan tixamin nnem Tinid kemm ur da ttarud Mami nezzwar ma g nedda 'Oajram' a 'Pascalani'... yubil nnem... seksuw Weyyel... Ma g tefferd? Tirsal nnem Tixxat nnem!! Tin Ulili tegrvaz am ur tlid... ma s tecneod! Baol-a-bakka ikk akwnd d ugari igenna Iuliy wafwa... Idgel nnem... "Svur" iri nnem pan irir nnem Ur idd iriri ghifngh... urar n ugherrabu deg Emporia Iufedv l "conges" deg Maurisia tenker d Ad taf asafar... waxxa izzvugher ... Tilelli nnek a amdvan... anurz nnek azarug... A tamurt a tasraft n iserdasen aghuln degm s tefrutin Zdemn kem ur kem tukiz tsertit Attuyn igudar.. teffegh truzvi Ur da ttighiyd s ixf nnem... Winnam irun winna idaqefn... teggeram d i tiggert Arraw nnem ur annayn Tajbirt i itersen nnem ula mayd iqqenn Iodawen nnem... ufan bab n tudert Akal... akwd iselli ganasent Ghifs ghrvesn i tudert Tidet gan d asfel inin tidet... Marur krezn a Nysa nnan rkezn!! Ugint tezziyawin... ggullant S iyeffasen s izvelmadven... ur nusiy Hemmezwura n wayucen gan d yiwn ... Imula n isvakanen lan taghwzi lan tirut Manig tesmulad..? (uhmad kessule, 10 /09/ 2006) 2002 Tawiza. .
