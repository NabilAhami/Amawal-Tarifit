 Uttvun 78, Mrayûr 2003 (Octobre 2003) Tamazight ? Tiittvawin nnem a Mazilya Yura t: Saois Oebbuti (Tifarsit) Tittvawin nnem a Mazilya, D azvri cem i twarid di reorasvi Netta ylellec s nnwac marra Ra x zermedv ra x ufusi. Abriwen nnem, a wtcma Nipni d tiwwura nni u seodi Mummu nnem a tanefsast D tfawt n ujenna war txessi Sadu tvya nnes nec ggamex Armi yedwer umcan war day ikessi. Tittvawin nnem a Mazilya D tcuni igi bdvan di reorasvi S wass tayyut ad terqqem zzay s S djiret tvram war xaf s itmarsi. Necc dewrex d ajdvidv n reorasvi Maca day s war day i treddi trussi Afriwen inu rebda nipni ttvawen Wur nnes s wazvri idwer ifessi. Ussan deg ûttvawen nnem Ggguren deghya am tmessi Deghya tmudjughen ghedjyen Di tmijja ynu amacnaw trussi. Aman di regoadi nnem a Mazilya Rebda tcurcuran d imizidven i tsessit Jj ayi a wetcma ad rahegh ad zedghegh Aylex ad ggex taddart di reorasvi Mayenzi tudart deg wydud nnex Teghdves amacnaw anu mebra tisi Rebda cem tuyred aked pekku d ttvuri Nix aorur nnec rebda xaf s tigharsi Rxezrat di tittvawin nnem a Mazilya Itmutti d anzvar anefsas war ykessi. Tudart day sent oawed d ijj n wawar Amazigh yarenni azvri wara d tyumi. (Di Tfarsit ass n 27 yunyu 2953) 2002 Tawiza. .
