 uïïun 157, smyur 2960 (Mai 2010) Tamazight Tast: Gar-muzzvel Yatru tt: Jebran xalil jebran, Asughel s tmazight: Agrawal afriqqic Nnix yat twalt i yan gar-muzzvel: - “Ized ur k tessvermi tzdught g iger ad g tellit ghas kiyyin, tegt gis usuf ?” Irar iy d iqqar: -“Pati ghuri g ussikes d yat timimt ur illi wanaw nnes, maya nn af iy tssusm twwuri inu, hmelx t” Swangemx yat talbqqiyt (imikk n wakud), sawlex d ilemma nnix as: -“Tghwzzvant g wawal nnek, acku izwar is uremx tidvfi ad s ixef inu, medvix t” Irar iy d inna: -“ad k is ar ttjijiwt, pati timimt ad ur issin tidvfi nnes amer wanna iddezen s iwreman am nekkin” Felx t ilemma akud ann, nefassix, meqqar ur ssinex is iy yulex neghd d is iy izzvimezviy. Izery usegwas n wadvan xef tizi ann, yaghul d Gar-muzzvel iga aneflus d yan g waggagen; azemz llig dagh ghifs zerix tikkelit yadven, dvufex nn yan sin igaywaren ar ukan sekkun isekki nnesn ddaw ubakeccu nnes. (Jebran xalil jebran, Zeg tawckint nnes: “Ikabaren d uhyudv”,Asughel s tmazight: Agrawal afriqqic, Anfa:14.06.2956) 2002 Tawiza. .
