 Uttvun 68, Meggyûr 2002 (Décembre 2002) Tamazight Netc tqrrigh awen (Je me confesse) Yaru t: Mmis n Izerruden (Tapla) Zzag i gher ixef inew Tar asefjeo Tqrrigh awen tura. Netc sxirreqegh xaf wen Deg iseggusa n izeryen Gherregh wen (wem) S wawliwen n yizvidven Ttighilegh d abrid n yecnan. Tura ini t akid i! Surf iyi a yakuc. Nnigh awen: Netc ghas ssawalegh Ku awal tettvefegh xafs chal Qao yetxllas it ukabar. A yaytma Awal inew texesen t ikabaren Netta yezvidv Ssehglayen t imezzughen, Maca cenwi (kenniw) a yaytma Lebda tesseghuyyum Terzvim azellif i wkabar Yya wen (yegga wem) tacmamt Yeqteo xaf wen awal Al d telmdem awal nnes. A yaytma Lmerruk yetmtta Ad as nessquttver aman Ad yejji, ad yekker Abeddi n yecnan. Oemmers ad yili Asafar n tmurt a Qao nnegh nessn it. Maca ifukk ad yeqqim yeffer, Huma ad yeqqim ukabar yedder, A yaytma Akabar nnegh yuss d s "ccero" d "lintixab" Ihekkem s taktabt Udji yetbba "lirpab" Netta zeg ugdud gher ugdud. A yaytma il iyi ttament! Netc ghas sxirriqehg xaf wen. 2002 Tawiza. .
