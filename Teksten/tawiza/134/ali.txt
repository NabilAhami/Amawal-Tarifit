 uïïun 134, sdyur 2958 (Juin 2008) Tamazight Iyyis Par: Ali Amaniss Nekka g tudert kigan n useggwas, Deghi nekka g tmizar akkw kigan, Ur n yad yagh iyyis nnegh arewas, Iyyis nnegh, awal ayd t yad igan. Netteca mayd righ ur i tegwerazv, Ur isul xes awal ad ilin g wammas, Ad as neg lanegri, ad t ur yagh lazv, Afad yan wass ay ixiter, ig arras. Ul innew isul yagh t yighneneka, Ur issin i tafessi, xes ar ten ya, Ira yawal ay iney deghi yat tnaka, Ir ay ig wawal g wul am isekwela. Awal nnegh ixater, ur issin lexela, Innemeyar aman ixataren d tnuya, Unna igan amazigh isul ay ya, Xef wawal, yan ay iga ammas n uy a. Iqqen neker akkw mas nella, A nerar xef wawal ass a d usekka, Afad ay ixiter, ad t ur tagh tawela, Winnegh ay iga, ur inni ay ig takka. 2002 Tawiza. .
