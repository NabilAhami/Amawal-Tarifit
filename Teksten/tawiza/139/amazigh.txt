 uïïun 139, ymrayur 2958 ( 2008) Tamazight Agheddu n nwar Yura t: Ahmed Azrak (Mdviq) A yagheddu n nwar d acemrar gi tbehhar n tadjest bedd ghar tadared i wsemmidv ghark ad tzedghed di tajrest i garin igar d aberkan amen ur ikmid... A yagheddu n nwar... uora deg ujenna am taziri! ssigh x tmawin n webrid ig tawin ameghnas amazigh ghar twafit n tusna n tmazight. Ay agheddu n nwar d ahmimi ireqhen, itiriden g itiri... dvu d tadbirt nigh d tasekwurt mmars x wurawen tesmun tayri arcem dagsen refrahet n umuni tizit n umuni.... 2002 Tawiza. .
