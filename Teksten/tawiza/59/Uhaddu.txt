 Uttvun 59, Krayûr 2002 (Mars 2002) Tamazight Radas war yeggîjen Yura t: Uhaddu Mhemmed (Zayyu) Twara t d anudem, twara t d axarresv di zman, Sdvaffaregh rxezrat yetnufsuren zeg ussan, Uyregh tent d aseqsi di radas war yeggîjen, Ssarsegh tent d araghi i yin yesniomiren, Araghi n tidett him ad ssnen. Ssnen nigh war ssinen, Oad ad yass uzir ad iban wi yeffaren. Wen yesqaren, ughir ad yessufegh iwaren. X anegh aghir, am neccin am iwdan nnidven. Zi rxezvrat n tiwecca yeccûren s iseqsiten, Aseqsi d araghi x wi yessvûrfen war yuci, Yessvûrf tudart n imezran isredd it ughimi, Aseqsi d rehriq n wur war yetgenfi, Idubbaz zi rebda war x as neggi mayemmi. Ahazi awarni tewwart neccin zi temzvi, Maca tidett tura, ijj war t imehhi. Tidett am tmûrt n temzvi i di yennefrura, Tidett am idvnnatv i d yenna jeddi, Tidett d neccin da zi mermi, Tarwa n tamazgha ughir x asen ad niri. Zayyu di 26/6/2951 2002 Tawiza. .
