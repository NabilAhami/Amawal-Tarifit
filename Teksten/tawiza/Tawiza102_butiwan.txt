 uïïun 102, mrayur 2005 (Octobre 2005) Tamazight I bu tiwan Yura t: Yukw zeg waraw n ughbalu n tserdan A ifsti urgh nna n i ayed tgit Maca unna yagh ca gaman adjjin Ad iznez ulli ns ad iznez imendi Ar irzzu ca n usafar ad t itgga. Ghas ad tnsim aoban ifley awn digh Ad dik tsin imoidan nna c idurn. Adj ad fsugh anghmes ad nna d ligh Unna i islan ad yi sin anig lligh Illa ughbalu n tserdan ghas iwerdac Max ur djin ad gh tghrit anemgraw?
