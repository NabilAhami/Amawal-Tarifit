 Uttvun 75, Sayûr 2003 (Juillet 2003) Tamazight Afrux inyer iberdan Yaru t: Ihya Muhemmed (Ayt Ishaq) Yix yan uferux amezzvan Tvewaoertex inyer iberdan Ur ssinex unna x ittveonan. Rix ad arux tamazight s isekkilen nna illan Tella tifinagh, tella taorabt, yili llatan. Mec d taorabt meqqar teni tadawet i lislam Taya asekkil ur ibunan Mec d llatan waxxa telaq Nnan tedda d zi berra n igherman Tifinagh ayed ax d iqqimen Mesasan ghifs ayt ugherbaz ageldan Gwdex ad zzenzin tasarut s yar ttaman Adjin bu igherman aresvli ad isettef iman. (Ayt Ishaq, 15/03/2003) 2002 Tawiza. .
