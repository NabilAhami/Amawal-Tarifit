 uïïun 133, smyur 2958 (Mai 2008) Tamazight Amour n ixef inu Mahmoud bellachir Cwayt zi rxezrat d cwayt zi raxart Ayezzim war ittu asendef, Ayezzim d babas n uxeyyeq, Advuqqezv d babas n tudart Jar tiwecca d idvennat, Nec farnegh abriwen inu zeg mettawen, Mahend ad afegh ca n wasagh Izemmar ad yidar tanfust inu.. Maca tiwecca d axarriq war ghars aneggar, Tiwecca d idvennat di tghuyyit inu.. Nec d memmis n mayemmi d ushinneb, Araji d anibu inu, d azedjid di turjitin inu.. Ca n ubarrih itesra yi d ruxa Ca n useqsi yarzzu xafi, Nec oad arezzugh x ca n wamour Ad ay afegh dayes? Minzi nec d udem n tarwra zi raxart, Tarwra zi tiwecca n temsses d ughras. Twarigh ruxa attvas n iseynan Ifetr iten usqar d icettihen.. Nec oad war ufigh amour Mani i gh afegh ixef inu.. (Mahmoud bellachir, qasita,Mahbell04@hotmail.com) 2002 Tawiza. .
