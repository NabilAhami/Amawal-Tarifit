 uïïun 153, ynyur 2960 (Janvier 2010) Tamazight asif n ighilufen Yura t: nizar qebbani, asughl s tmazight: agrawal afriqqic tiwin nnem: sin isaffen n ighilufn sin isaffen n uzawan.. s darat rurniy yi.. s darat n izemaz.. , yap ssaghulniy yi sin isaffen n uzawan jelanin a la.. semden jluniy yi..! ar ittar g iggi nsen amettva idili zund apyanu ittirirn alaydalali..! tiwin nnem d garru inu d unziz, kkesn iy izeri d akus wiss meraw g taqqayt ig izeri nekkin da yjddern g usggawr takat inu ar tctta ixf nes ur tgawr a tamarayt inu, rixkem, is rad t inix? axxayt axxayt mera s ufix..! acku g umadval ur dari yat amer tiwin nnem d ighilufn inu, gin snat..! tighrruba inu g uftas ar ant f iggi n tilletatin ar gezzment.. isennsetmiy yi awragh ad igan amesdul tafelst g idmarn inu ur sul tsul is rad mmuddux min kemmin? ayamalu n wakuc g tittvawin a yanebdu azegzaw inu, a tafukt g iklan inu tafalkayt ad tuft.. is d iqqan ad nn gigm ittix?! meqqar tuf aghul n ibriry test negh ad inix meqqar tuf gardinya ajddig g izlan isppanyuln ammas n yid ?! ad ur tat a badad inu igan yan imettvawn nem skern yad g ugwns inu igwdyan.. acku ur dari g umadval yat amer tiwin nnem d ighilufn inu gin snat..! a tamarayt inu, rixkem? is rad t inix ?! axxayt axxayt mera’s ufix..! nekkin da’ygan afgan iwwujdan i wmeras inu g wakal ur gix ameyassan abrid inu ad iy ijlan.. assagh inu ad iy ijlan.. tansa inu ad’iy ijlan.. amezruy?! mad t innan?! nekkin war amezruy.. tatawt inu , tatawt nes ur ar t tezzruy nekkin da ygan aftas infattasn aggas g tifras ufgan.. ma’rad am fkex? is gigm madd ittraran?! anughejf inu, tartaflest, negh dd iraran?! ma rad am fkex amer amesdul irekkezn g tidiklt n ccitan yimyur.. nekkin da ygan ifed n rixkem, ittin gigi takat d garru- inu, am d ur kkin ddaw ula iggi acku ur dari g umadal awd yat amer tiwin nnem d ighilufn inu, gin snat..! (di 14 – 10 - 2958) 2002 Tawiza. .
