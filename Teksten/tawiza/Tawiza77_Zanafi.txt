 Uttvun 77, Tzayûr 2003 ( 2003) Tamazight Tinw ayenna Yaru t: Oumar Zanafi Adimun (Asif n Dades Tin Mulley) Ah a nekkin Immutern tillas ad ghelint Taghelya n tillas ad d zwint. Ah a nekkin Mi yufen-t tin yit acku llant Tin uzal ityuskern ag oetant. Ah a nekkin Yutemn i wmalu ag digs lligh Gegh abrid i tillas, tafuyt ad neghigh. Ah a nekkin Itsemmaren atar, ayenna yga yemmut Fkegh iman ad ssidergh ighes ittuten Ma mi ttinigh tinew Idd nekkin ayccewan ajenwiy iyghersen Nekkin aykettun isker itcan imi new Iwegh n tillas ag qejjigh ils inew Talbbuzet n wussan ag bercegh afus inew Timzin n ufus inew as i yiwt unugut inew Afus dda fekigh, ad id igern xef ighef inew. 2002 Tawiza. .
