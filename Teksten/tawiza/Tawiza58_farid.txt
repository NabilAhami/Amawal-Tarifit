 58, (Février 2002) Tamazight Tnayen n isekkûrn Yura t: Farid Lxalqi (Pulanda) Uyûr ad niri d tnayen n isekkûrn, Ad names rhvenni i tfednin n idvaren, Ad narr ussan nnegh d reoyud ttemsedvfaren, A nbedd x yesri i tugha d ane gh yeffaren, Ad nejj reodu nnegh marra day negh d xzvzaren. Waxxa war nettviw, gza anegh ijj n wemcan, Ad niri nec d ccem war dji wi d anegh gha ad yebdvan. Uyûr ad niri d tnayen isekkûrn, Ad names rhvenni i tfednin n idvarn, Ad nbedd d ijj n wedrar jar tmûrt d yetran. War t’ tiryen imnusen war t’ tcuqin ifeqsan. Ad nari ijj n umezruy war dji wi t’ gh ad yemhan. Uyûr ad niri d tnayen isekkûrn, Ad names rhvenni i tfednin n idvarn, Ad nejj ijj n yezri d ajdjid x yezran, Ad as negg nec d ccem, a yedji s n imezghan, Ass i gh ad mtegh ghar it cem x imedvran. 2002 Tawiza. .
