 Uttvun 79, Yemrayûr 2003 ( 2003) Tamazight Tarusi (Amsedvfar) Yaru t: Muhemmed Ihya (Ayt Ishaq, tamesmunt Imal) …Yiwedv wakud nna xef msasan. Kker ammas n yidv, meqqar g udghar nna yan. Usin d dduzan ad sen ilaqen. Llan s cradv, lfqip d sin n imeddukal nes. Lefqip inna sen pan ca digun ad isiwel ghen d ad ipbedv ad ax zlin ayt rebbi. Iwdven gher Terwilal: ansa nna gher ddan. Sersen ihrwijen nsen. Isfal lefqip ar iteqra. Asin wiyyadv igenzam ar qqazen. Yan cwi iffegh d ghur sen yan iydi abexxan. Imil awed yuk diksen immuten zeg udghar nes, ur ttiwin g walu. Zayden ar seghbun ahfur. Lefqip isul da iteqra ar iserri tidi. Yuk zeg imeddukal nes iwehel, iqqim ar itesghunfa. Cwi sellan i ca ittviqs, yasi waddax iqqiman, ar iferredv acal iy iffegh gher yat tqellalt, nettat ayd irzva bu ugenzim zeg imi. Ssufghen t id, afin tirra. Lefqip ur yukiz awed hah mec as temzyald d taorabt, wiyyadv ur qerarn ur sin walu. Usin taqllalt is ftun gher igherman nsen gher tifawet yusi d yuk diksen talwit ddax, inna sen i waraw nes nna iqran ciyyan: man digun ayed iqran tacinwit ittujd ad iy qra can tirra da inna s umenzu yumzven turayt g taorabt: taoibrit n wudayen aynna a bba, ineqqez d umezzvan zi teghmert n taddart inna sen: patin tifinagh aynna. Tirra n tmazight. Patin nerqra t i tmesmunt nnay ammux, nedda ad… Amawal: Tarusi: trésor - Akud: le temps - tirwilal: tawrirt illan g ayt Ishaq nnay gan irumin lqecla nsen afella nes - Isgunfa: se reposer - leqredv: talwit = morceau - turayt: la licence. 2002 Tawiza. .
