 Uttvun 83, Krayûr 2004 (Mars 2004) Tamazight Timawayin Iseman t: Lhusin Dmami (Warzazat). Tazewaret: timawayin ddegh ur tteyassanen id bab-nnesenet,ula maghef tteyannanet; ghes tameggarut, inna yan ureyaz mi immut useredun (aweras) g "agedal-n-mejedi" ger "dades"d "buyemmaz" g" azilal". Tamegharet ddatenet ioaweden, tega seg igherem n "ayet yul" g taqebilet n"ayet sedderat-n-yighil”, g temaziret n"bumalen-dades", g "warezazat". Isem nnes "la dapa oemer". tamawayet tamezewarut: awissen a rebbi, aghulid imeneoen, isetat ur ifeki tvemeo anali Ad as nettec ighera i tizewa? gregh alen s-nnigi, isiweled yan benadem, innayi:"seg maddi nekeregh ayedi tezela lepem qqumigh nnigas, nusi d tasekkaleya tegezilagh, nebab aseghun zvares ur lekimen. ioema xir llap, yiri aselal n-tizzewa, Ur t itteci ghes wadda g tella nniyet Yitvefi t, rewun a sidi g-uqemu, ur sarak irezvim ughulid a bab n tehellalet". tamawayet tis senat: Ufigh t id g yan ugeredv ar texemmamegh ad ten svumegh. i wellap d aberrad aoecari uras ilaq ghes adilin g tagwuset ilin ghur ttvawes isudda watay nnes yiweyagh lexadver ar ttinigegh tizi nnes iged ufigh ageru a svinit iji n kwun ighemayam irekan gwulligh atay urt natvi. Tamawayet tis kratv: datni neggan, awin adagh yagh i remigh g itves, ikesit uyellid rebbi. i zerighen aweras g ammas n yidv s yan ur digesen izedigh illa yizem d ugayewar i nebuba axeridv, negad am kera g lemesakin Niwi d idv zvarun a "buyemmaz", ifekayaghed "uoemum" atay awin adi ghelin ar netteneddap zvarun a "sidi bu lexelef" aterarim ilegimen kullu lejedid awer ususen. 2002 Tawiza. .
