 Uttvun 83, Krayûr 2004 (Mars 2004) Tamazight Ini as i wmazigh Yaru t: Lekbir Lghazi (Aghbalu n Tsrdant) Nek Ini as i wmazigh ighuda c wadvu, Ur iqqim mac itggan izwar, Amm lli g ar issvan. Ttuganget tmesmunin, illa umussu, Amazigh nsla s. Ittuga usinag amazigh gh nannit Illa qah "lghar" ur idd ay tannid! Ghas winna ibibban g tadawt a gwa, Ayed ittugan dik s ur illi gar ngetta. Ttuga tinmel nanni jaj nes tirra, N tfinagh, yaru t umazigh s ufus. Ixf in Ini as i wmazigh ixub ac wadvu Tgid tin usrdunt d bab nec walut. Ghas unna kwen irran iddu d akw yawi Addag ic fukkan addag ur c iri. Irzvm ac izvoukkwen a yamazigh fafa! Mani tinmel ghas ttugas s onwa Akw ur illi idles i winna day isghuran! Berra n "la loi" ag ittuga umata. Mani "ddastara" n tmazight ass ad Ma ur illi izref amm twada n tillas Gudinet tifcay i widda it ddunt. 2002 Tawiza. .
