 Uttvun 83, Krayûr 2004 (Mars 2004) Tamazight Tagrutt nna iran ad tizir anect n ufunas Tarurt n Hha Oudadess (Rrbatv) Yat tagrutt tannay yan ufunas. Takez ghur s tiddi ighudan. Nttat ur izuren akkw amm taglayt, Tasddarnutt, tennughzef, taff, temmenzagh, Ad nn tagh tazzert n umuder. Ar ttini: “Zêrê, zêrê, ah ultma; Idd iwda? Ini yi, is urta? -Upu. - Piwa pa yyi diges? -Akkw upu. Pa yyinn diges? -Ur akkw nn tenmalat“. Tamesdadet tamennefalt, Taff akkw ig tettîqqes. Amadval itkwar s midden ur yad igin imesnawen: Ku aneghrvam ixes ad ibnu zund imgharven, Ku mesgellid mzzviyen ila ibermazanen, Ku amessudu ira ad yili imedfarven. (Rrebadê 01-11-97) 2002 Tawiza. .
