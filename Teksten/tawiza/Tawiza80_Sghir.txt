 Uttvun 80, Meggyûr 2003 (Décembre 2003) Tamazight Atbir Amggrad Yaru t: Oumar Svghir (Assif n Dades, Ayt Oebdun) A yatbir amggrad trurt s iggnwan tzrit ifrexan ar an gg ighrman ur asen ikkis imtti lazv wala ighuyyan arapa yan ur ssin matn yaghen ig selsan fghen si brdan assin iggldan saflla ran imenghan. ****** A yatbir amggrad mqqar oetan ioedawen tkkit nigg ighuliden ibddan ur kw lkwimen ifasen n imhsaden ur kw anayent walen n inggmaren u rkw saghen waldyyen n imkwsawn. ***** A yatbir amggrad mqqar oetan ioedawen n tudert wala uccan Tawrirt n Musa ad k nghan maca tgit ayyur ammas n itran ar tssiddit ibrdan n yan diddan. (omar sghir ass n 28-05-2003 di Agadir) amawal amggrad: pigeon voyageur -ifrexan: (afrux) petit du pigeon - lazv: faim - inggemarn: (anggmar) chasseur - waledyen: lance pierre ou fronde - imkwsawen - (amkwsa) bergé ou pasteur. 2002 Tawiza. .
