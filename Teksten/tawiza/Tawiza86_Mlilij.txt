 Uttvun 86, Sedyur 2004 (Juin 2004) Tamazight Mlilij (Asemdu) Yaru t: Oumar Sghir Dda yed yaghul digh ttvir inna s d mhemd i mlilij mas delt matssit a mlilij… tnna s delgh stezrbay ssughtent a mhemd inu. Ilin itsen da bnnun ard kmmiln itersasend ttvir ghef uyadir inna sen: nnepdem ama tbnam ayi bnnayen… inepdm asen kullu mad bnan. Ddun ar ttiniggn mad skaren imih saqsan yan wuday oawdn as kullu mad asen iskar ttvir inna sen: oamrat tafnna (cefna) n nhas s tamimt tegm dis itsen inzaden gg dinna y itras. Ddun igg sekern aylligh ad sen inna wuday imih iddud digh ttvir ira ay ters a flla n uyadir tamz as tamimit iyldan igg ur ighiy a yayel, imih ittverd dis mhemd iga zund aydvitv s laz ighulnd ayt igherm lligh nnan asen i meden mer tezrim yan urba ittverd aggensu n ttvir teslayasen mlilij ard tazla tennasen: Pat uyma aynnagh… mhemd uyma inu.. Fkat itid. tawit s taddart aras tikka kan teglay d lhlib iy ijji. (FIN) 2002 Tawiza. .
